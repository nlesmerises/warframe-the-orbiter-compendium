🔥 1. The Damage & Status Meta

🦠 The Ultimate Combo: Viral + Slash

This is the most powerful damage combination in Warframe, capable of killing level 9999 enemies. Here is how it works:

  • Viral (Toxin + Cold): The Viral status effect increases damage dealt to enemy HEALTH by up to +325%. It does not strip armor.
  • Slash (Physical): The Slash status effect causes the enemy to bleed. Slash bleeding deals "True Damage," which completely ignores enemy armor.
  • The Synergy: The Slash bleed ignores the enemy's armor and attacks their health directly. The Viral proc multiplies that bleed damage by 325%. The enemy dies instantly, regardless of how much armor they have.

⚗️ The Secondary Combo: Corrosive + Heat

If you have a weapon that cannot inflict Slash procs (like a Rocket Launcher or Beam weapon), this is the combo you use instead against Grineer or Infested.

  • Corrosive (Toxin + Electric): The Corrosive status effect strips away up to 80% of an enemy's armor permanently.
  • Heat (Fire): The Heat status effect sets enemies on fire (panic CC), deals damage over time, and strips up to 50% of their armor.
  • The Synergy: Corrosive + Heat completely melts armored targets by aggressively stripping their defenses away so your raw damage can break through.

🔥 Other Key Damage Types

🧲 Magnetic (Cold + Electric)

The ultimate anti-shield element. It increases damage dealt to shields by up to +325%. Essential for fighting high-level Corpus units.

☢️ Radiation (Heat + Electric)

Causes enemies to become confused and shoot each other (disabling Eximus auras). It is also the #1 element for fighting Eidolons and Bosses, as most boss armor in the game is "Alloy Armor," which is incredibly weak to Radiation.

🔬 2. The Focus Schools

Once you unlock the Operator, you can earn Focus points to level up skill trees. You can only have one School active at a time.

⚡ Zenurik (The Energy School)

The most popular school for general gameplay. Unlocking Wellspring allows your Operator to create a bubble that grants massive Energy Regeneration to you and your Warframe for 30+ seconds. Essential for caster frames.

👑 Madurai (The Damage School)

The Eidolon/Boss hunting school. Grants massive flat damage buffs to physical and elemental damage. Unlocking Void Strike gives your Operator a massive burst of damage to instantly break Eidolon shields.

⚔️ Naramon (The Melee School)

The melee combo school. Unlocking Power Spike changes the melee combo counter so that instead of resetting to zero after a few seconds, it only decays by 5 combo hits at a time. Essential for Blood Rush/Weeping Wounds melee builds.

🛡️ Vazarin / Unairu

Vazarin: The healing school. Unlocking Protective Sling allows your Operator to dash through your Warframe (or allies/defense pods) to heal them and grant them 5 seconds of total invincibility.

Unairu: The armor strip school. Caustic Strike permanently strips 100% of enemy armor in an AOE.

🧲 4. Mod Capacity & Forma Quick Reference

📌 Capacity Rules

  • Base capacity: 30 at rank 0 → 60 at rank 30
  • Orokin Reactor/Catalyst: Doubles capacity (60 → 120)
  • Matching polarity slot: Halves mod drain (round up)
  • Mismatched slot: +25% additional drain on top of base
  • Forma: Resets rank to 0, adds/changes one polarity slot
  • Umbra Forma: Applies Umbral polarity — any slot, any weapon

🎯 Key Build Thresholds

  • Crit Chance: 150–200% for consistent orange crits
  • Status Chance: 100% = proc every shot; 200% = two procs per shot
  • Ability Strength: Most frames cap useful gains at 200–300%
  • Armor (EHP): Gains slow past ~3,000 armor — formula-based DR
  • Fire Rate: No DR but animation locks create practical ceiling

💡 6. How Focus Actually Works

🔎 Lenses & Earning Focus

  • Unlocked by the The War Within quest — see the Quest Codex.
  • Install a Lens on a rank-30 Warframe or weapon to convert a share of its affinity into Focus for one school.
  • Lens tiers: Lens → Greater Lens → Eidolon Lens (each converts more). Use Eidolon Lenses on your farming frame.
  • Convergence Orbs spawn from kills — grabbing one multiplies Focus gain massively for ~45s. Dump affinity during the window.
  • Focus earned per day is subject to a daily cap; come back tomorrow once capped.

🔗 Active School vs. Way-Bound

  • You equip one active school at a time — its Operator ability (Wellspring, Void Strike, etc.) only works while it's active.
  • Way-Bound nodes are account-wide passives that stay on no matter which school is active.
  • You can bind Way-Bounds from every school — so unlock the best passives across all five even if you only main one.
  • Each school has its own Focus pool to fill; eventually max them all.

📖 7. Per-School Deep Dive

🔥 Madurai — Damage

  • Active: Void Strike — charge while in Void Mode, then unleash a stacked Amp damage burst.
  • Key nodes: Sling Strength, Void Radiance (blind on Void Sling).
  • Way-Bound: Rising Blast, Phoenix Spirit (Amp/weapon damage).
  • Best for: Eidolon shield removal, raw Operator/Amp DPS.

⚡ Zenurik — Energy

  • Active: Wellspring — energy-regen zone for you and your Warframe.
  • Key nodes: Energy Pulse (bonus from Energy Orbs), Temporal Drag.
  • Way-Bound: Inner Might (heavy-attack / channel efficiency) — near-mandatory pickup.
  • Best for: The default for general play & caster frames.

✚ Vazarin — Defense

  • Active: Protective Sling — dash through allies / defense objectives / Eidolon Lures to heal & grant brief invulnerability.
  • Key nodes: Mending Soul (charges), Guardian Shell.
  • Way-Bound: Mending Unity, Sage's Wisdom.
  • Best for: Defense/escort, keeping Eidolon Lures alive, squad support.

⚔️ Naramon — Melee

  • Active: Disorienting Blast (CC) — but the draw is the passive.
  • Key node / Way-Bound: Power Spike — melee combo decays in small steps instead of dropping to zero.
  • Also: Affinity Spike, Mind Step.
  • Best for: Blood Rush / Weeping Wounds combo-scaling melee builds.

🛡️ Unairu — Armor Strip

  • Active: Caustic Strike — Void Sling permanently strips enemy armor in an area.
  • Key nodes: Magnetic Flare (CC + shield strip), Unairu Wisp, Poise (knockdown immunity).
  • Way-Bound: Basilisk Scales, Stone Skin.
  • Best for: Steel Path armor stripping without a strip frame; strong, modern pick.

🎯 Quick Pick

  • New player: Zenurik (energy) first.
  • Eidolons: Madurai active + Unairu/Vazarin support.
  • Melee main: Naramon for Power Spike.
  • Steel Path: Unairu for free armor strip.
  • Then bind the best Way-Bounds from all schools.

🔭 8. Farming Focus & Lenses

📌 Best Focus Farms

Elite Sanctuary Onslaught (ESO) with a high-kill frame (Saryn, Gauss) and Eidolon Lenses is the classic grind — massive affinity + frequent Convergence. Eidolon hunts and any dense kill mission (Hydron, survival) also work. Always pop Convergence Orbs before unloading affinity.

🔬 Getting Lenses

Eidolon Lens blueprints come from Quills standing (Cetus, post-Saya's Vigil); craft Greater→Eidolon. Regular Lenses appear in Nightwave, Sortie, and login rewards. Put your best lenses on the frame/weapon you farm with most.

📌 Bind These Way-Bounds First

Inner Might (Zenurik), Mending Unity (Vazarin), Rising Blast (Madurai). Account-wide value regardless of your active school.

💡 Don't Forget Operator Gear

Pair schools with Magus/Virtuos arcanes and a good Amp — the Arcane Codex covers the staples (Magus Elevate/Lockdown, Virtuos Strike).

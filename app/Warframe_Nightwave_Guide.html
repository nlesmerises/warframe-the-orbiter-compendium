📻 1. What Is Nightwave?

📻 Series vs. Intermission

  • Series: Themed story arc with unique cosmetics and lore (limited time)
  • Intermission: Filler season between Series — same mechanics, no story
  • Nora Night narrates events via radio transmissions during missions
  • No time-gate on individual challenges — complete at your own pace within the season

⭐ Standing & Ranks

  • Complete Acts to earn Nightwave Standing
  • Standing fills a rank bar — each rank unlocks a reward tier
  • Prestige ranks continue past max rank for additional NW Credits
  • NW Credits carry over between seasons — they do not expire

📋 2. Challenge Types

📅 Daily Acts (1,000 Standing)

Refresh every 24 hours. Typically simple tasks: kill X enemies with a damage type, complete a mission on a specific node, craft something, or interact with game systems. Always complete these — they stack up fast.

📆 Weekly Acts (3,000–5,000 Standing)

Refresh every 7 days. More involved: complete 3 Nightmare missions, run a Sortie, earn 5,000 Mastery, complete Syndicate missions, etc. Higher Standing per act than Dailies.

🏆 Elite Weekly Acts (7,000–10,000 Standing)

The best Standing per act. Typically harder content: complete an Arbitration, run a 60-minute survival, complete all 3 Sorties, etc. Always prioritize these — they can carry you through multiple ranks solo.

🎯 Strategy: Prioritization

Do Elite Weeklies first, then regular Weeklies, then Dailies as filler. You do not need to complete every act to max the season — Elite Weeklies alone cover most of the required Standing.

🛍 3. NW Credit Store — What to Buy

⭐ Highest Priority

  • Umbra Forma Blueprint — only source; essential for Umbra builds
  • Aura Forma Blueprint — upgrades aura slot to accept any aura mod
  • Arcane Energize — top energy Arcane; expensive elsewhere
  • Nitain Extract — limited resource used in many Warframe crafts

🌟 Medium Priority

  • Vauban / Wolf Sledge / any Warframe parts in the store
  • Weapon and Warframe slots — always useful
  • Season-exclusive cosmetics (Syandanas, Armor sets) — time-limited
  • Orokin Reactors / Catalysts if you are running low

🔵 Low Priority / Skip

  • Forma Bundles — obtainable through normal gameplay and Nightwave drops
  • Credit Cache — Credits are plentiful endgame (Index, Profit-Taker)
  • Common mods and resources available in normal missions

🔧 4. Tricky Acts & Solutions

⚖️ Complete an Arbitration

You must unlock every node on the star chart first. Run Arbitration Defense (Lith, Earth) for an easy clear — bring a tank Warframe like Inaros or Revenant. You only need to complete one rotation.

💀 Complete 3 Nightmare Missions

Any Nightmare node on the star chart counts. Pick the fastest mission type (Capture or Exterminate) on lower-level nodes. Nightmare modifiers stack — build accordingly or just power through.

🎯 Kill X Eximus Enemies

Mot Void Survival or any Steel Path mission spawns Eximus units frequently. Run for 10+ minutes to rack up kills efficiently. Disruption tiles in high-level nodes also spawn many Eximus.

👥 Complete Missions with a Full Squad

Use Recruit chat (/r) or go public on popular nodes (Helene Saturn, Hydron Sedna). Alternatively, invite clan mates or alliance members. Any mission with 4 players at the end of extraction counts.

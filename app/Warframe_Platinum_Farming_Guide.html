📦 1. The Relic Hustle

Prime Parts

This is the most fundamental way to make platinum in Warframe. You run Void Fissure missions to crack Relics and obtain Prime blueprints/parts.

🪙 Selling “Prime Junk”

If you get common bronze/silver drops that nobody wants, do not worry! Other players buy these specifically to turn them into Ducats for Baro Ki'Teer. You can consistently sell 6 random Prime parts for 10-15 Platinum.

🪙 Selling Prime Sets

If you farm all 4 parts of a Warframe (Blueprint, Neuroptics, Chassis, Systems), you can sell it as a "Full Set." Popular frame sets (like Wisp Prime or Volt Prime) can sell for anywhere between 50 to 150+ Platinum.

🔓 2. Corrupted Mod Farming

The Most Consistent

This is the most reliable way to make platinum in the early-to-mid game.

  • How to do it: Build all 4 Dragon Keys in your Dojo. Equip them to your gear wheel. Run the Horend (Capture) node on Deimos. Find the glowing Orokin Vault door, open it, and finish the mission.
  • What you get: Corrupted Mods.
  • The Payout: If you get Blind Rage, Transient Fortitude, Narrow Minded, Fleeting Expertise, or Overextended, you can instantly sell them on Warframe.Market for 15 to 20 Platinum each. You can do a run in 2 minutes!

💸 3. Passive Income

Syndicates

Pledge to a Syndicate (like Steel Meridian or Arbiters of Hexis) at the console in your Orbiter. As you play the game normally, you will earn standing with them.

🃏 Augment Mods

Once you reach max rank, you can buy Warframe Augment Mods for 25,000 standing. You can sell highly desired augments (like Nekros' Despoil or Khora's Pilfering Strangledome) for 10 to 15 Platinum to players who don't want to grind that syndicate.

🤝 Syndicate Weapons

You can buy Syndicate Weapons (like the Vaykor Hek) for 100,000 standing and sell them completely unranked to other players for 30 to 50 Platinum.

📈 4. The Riven Market

High Risk / High Reward

As you enter the late game, you will get "Veiled Riven Mods" from Daily Sorties, Archon Hunts, and from Palladino in Iron Wake.

🔑 The Safe Route (Selling Veiled)

Do not open the Riven. Simply sell the "Veiled Melee Riven Mod" or "Veiled Rifle Riven Mod" as-is. They usually sell for 15 to 30 Platinum guaranteed.

🎲 The Gambler's Route (Unveiling)

Complete the challenge to unveil the Riven. If it rolls for a terrible weapon (like the Stug), it's worth zero plat. If it rolls for a meta weapon (like the Torid, Glaive, or Nataruk), you can sell it for 100 to 500+ Platinum even with bad stats. If you reroll it with Kuva and get "God Tier" stats... you can sell it for thousands.

⚠️ 5. Arbitration Mods

Endgame Hustle

Once you clear the Star Chart, play Arbitration missions to earn Vitus Essence.

  • Take your Vitus Essence to the Arbiters of Hexis in any Relay.
  • Buy copies of Galvanized Chamber, Galvanized Aptitude, or Rolling Guard.
  • Sell them on Warframe.Market for 15 to 25 Platinum each. This is highly consistent because every single new player needs these mods to survive the Steel Path!

💀 1. Overview

Kuva Lich (Grineer)

Grineer
  • Prerequisite: complete The War Within quest
  • Created by mercy-killing a Larvling in any Lv 20+ Grineer mission
  • Rewards: Kuva weapons with a random bonus elemental damage type
  • Converted Liches can be deployed as Railjack crew members

Sister of Parvos (Corpus)

Corpus
  • Prerequisite: complete The Deadlock Protocol quest
  • Created by mercy-killing a Candidate on Venus Proxima (Railjack) or Corpus nodes
  • Rewards: Tenet weapons with a random bonus element
  • Converted Sisters act as Railjack crew and can be traded

🔍 2. Creating Your Lich / Sister

⚠️ Weapon Choice Matters

The weapon shown on the Larvling or Candidate determines which Kuva/Tenet weapon your nemesis drops. Do not mercy-kill if the weapon displayed is not one you want — leave the mission and retry. The Larvling will despawn without a mercy kill.

📊 Bonus Element & Percentage

At creation, a random element is assigned (Heat, Cold, Electric, Toxin, Magnetic, Radiation, Viral, Impact). The bonus is 25–60% of total weapon damage. Aim for 55%+ — higher % rolls are significantly stronger. Ephemera has ~20% chance based on element type.

🔑 3. Requiem Mods — The Parazon Code

You must discover the correct 3-mod Requiem sequence by farming Murmurs. Each wrong mercy attempt levels up your Lich (making it harder) until the sequence is found. Wrong mods or wrong order deal no damage.

🃏 The 8 Requiem Mods

  • Ris, Fass, Vome, Netra — base set
  • Jahu, Xata, Khra, Lohk — advanced set
  • Obtained from Kuva Siphon and Kuva Flood missions
  • Each mod has limited charges — they are consumed on use (right or wrong)

🔫 Parazon & Mercy Kill

  • Equip discovered Requiem mods on your Parazon (gear tab)
  • Down the Lich in a mission to attempt a mercy kill
  • Correct sequence → Vanquish or Convert choice
  • Wrong sequence → Lich survives, levels up, flees

🌿 4. Murmur Farming

How Murmurs Work

Murmur
  • Kill Thralls (Lich-controlled enemies) in missions to fill the Murmur meter
  • 3 Murmur bars total — each filled bar reveals one Requiem mod slot
  • Thralls spawn on nodes your Lich controls (marked with Lich symbol)
  • Your Lich may appear mid-mission — fight it or flee to another node

Efficient Strategy

Murmur
  • Run missions on nodes your Lich controls — more Thrall spawns
  • Do not attempt mercy until all 3 Requiem slots are revealed
  • Wrong mercy = Lich levels up (Lv 1→5 max) — harder to kill
  • Co-op: other players' Thralls also fill your Murmur bar

Convert vs. Vanquish

Murmur
  • Vanquish: Lich dies, you receive its weapon + ephemera immediately
  • Convert: Lich becomes a Railjack crew member ally
  • Converted Liches can be traded at Crimson Branch in your Dojo
  • Trading transfers the weapon + ephemera to the buyer

🔥 8. Bonus Element Reference

Bonus element % is added on top of the base weapon’s damage. For weapons with innate elements, the bonus element can combine with the innate one (e.g., Kuva Nukor with Toxin + innate Radiation = Corrosive in practice).

Bonus % Tiers

Elements
Range Quality Notes
25–34%PoorReroll or trade away
35–44%Below AvgAcceptable early on
45–54%AverageUsable, keep for now
55–59%GoodRecommended minimum for endgame
60%Max RollBest possible — trade value is high

Best Elements by Use Case

Elements
  • Heat — universal pick, armor strip + DoT. Best all-rounder for Grineer
  • Toxin — excellent for Corpus (bypasses shields). Pairs with cold for Viral
  • Electricity — good for Corpus robots; contributes to Corrosive or Magnetic combos
  • Radiation — best for Sentient and Alloy-armored Grineer heavies
  • Magnetic — top pick for dedicated Corpus shield-shredding builds
  • Ephemera unlock: each element type has its own associated ephemera cosmetic (~20% chance at vanquish)

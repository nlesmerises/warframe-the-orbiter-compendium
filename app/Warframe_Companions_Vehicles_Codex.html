🐾 1. Pets & Sentinels

🦊 Panzer Vulpaphyla

Immortal Viral Pet

Acquired in the Cambion Drift (Deimos). Spreads Viral spores automatically to target groups. When it dies, it goes into a larva form and respawns 30 seconds later.

🃏 Core Mods

  • Viral Quills (Mandatory): Viral spores on hit.
  • Panzer Devolution (Mandatory): Immortality link.
  • Fetch (Loot Vacuum)
  • Animal Instinct (Radar)

🔗 Build Synergies

Because the Panzer handles Viral propagation across the room, you can free up weapon mod slots to build for pure Corrosive/Heat or raw physical scaling.

🐱 Smeeta Kavat

Loot Multiplier

Bred in the Incubator. Extremely valuable pet for grinding resources, Void Traces, and Steel Path resources because of its Charm multiplier.

🃏 Core Mods

  • Charm (Mandatory): Grants double resource buffs.
  • Tek Enhance (Extends buff durations)
  • Fetch (Vacuum)
  • Animal Instinct (Radar)

📋 Playstyle Rule

Equip when harvesting resources. When the Charm affinity icon is active on your dashboard, claim high-value nodes or crystals immediately to double outputs.

🤖 Carrier Prime

Ammo Conversion

Sentinel providing massive utility to explosive armaments, weapon platforms with high fire rates, and general ammo-hungry gear.

🃏 Core Mods

  • Ammo Case (Mandatory): Universal ammo conversion.
  • Looter: Automatically shoots storage boxes.
  • Vacuum & Guardian

🔗 Synergy Option

Equip with Primed Firestorm setups to guarantee constant ammo regeneration. Best for weapons with low ammo pool reserves.

🚀 2. Vehicles

🦾 Voidrig Necramech

Necramech Nuke

Heavy weapons platform essential for Open World bounties and endgame Orphix fields. Trivializes heavy target battles.

🃏 Core Mods

  • Vitality & Fiber (Reinforcements)
  • Streamline & Flow (Energy scaling)
  • Repair (Incidental healing)

🔫 Arquebex Cannons

Activating Guard Mode pulls out dual artillery cannons. Mod them for Corrosive/Heat with massive critical damage mods to deal millions of DPS.

🛸 Amesha Archwing

Immortal Support

The absolute top-tier Archwing utility. Provides total invincibility, friendly healing zones, and converts incoming damage into energy.

🃏 Core Mods

  • System Reroute (Efficiency - Key)
  • Enhanced Durability (Max Health)
  • Argon Plating (Armor rating)

🔄 Ability Loop

Cast 1 to shield against direct hits. Place 2 on static defense items to heal. Use 4 when low on energy to sponge damage and refill energy immediately.

🏆 4. Top Companions by Role

🌟 Best for Farming

  • Smeeta Kavat — Charm proc can double resource drops, double affinity, or replicate pickups
  • Carrier Prime — Vacuum + Ammo Case keeps ammo topped off passively
  • Sahasa Kubrow — Dig precept uncovers buried loot on the ground

🎯 Best for Combat

  • Panzer Vulpaphyla — Viral Quills applies Viral to nearby enemies passively
  • Adarza Kavat — Cat’s Eye gives +60% crit chance to you on proc
  • Helios Prime — Investigator auto-scans codex entries while you fight

🛡️ Best for Survival

  • Panzer Vulpaphyla — Immortal via larva revive loop; best endgame companion
  • Raksa Kubrow — Howl repels enemies + shield restore on command
  • Medjay Predasite — Immune to death loop; ancient-like revive mechanic

  • K-Drive: Hover board for open worlds — earns Ventkids standing, no combat use

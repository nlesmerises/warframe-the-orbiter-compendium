🥇 Tier 1: The Absolute Best (Endgame Squads)

Pre-Made Squad
  • Vodyanoi, Sedna (Rathuum Arena)
    ~2,000 to 3,000 Endo per 2 minutes. This is the undisputed king of raw Endo farming. You need a dedicated, pre-made squad: Nidus (to group enemies), Khora (Pilfering Strangledome), and 2x Nekros (Desecrate). Everyone must run purely Slash weapons (like Ripkas with Amalgam Ripkas True Steel) to dismember bodies and double the Desecrate drops. Mod drop chance boosters affect Endo drops here!

🥈 Tier 2: The Best Solo / High-Yield Alternatives

Solo Friendly
  • Zariman & Cavia Bounties
    ~3,000 to 4,000 Endo per run. High-tier bounties on the Zariman Ten Zero and the Sanctum Anatomica often drop massive 3,000+ Endo bundles as their common or uncommon rewards. If a fast mission (like Exterminate) has Endo in its reward pool, spamming it repeatedly is incredibly fast and entirely soloable.
  • Railjack Skirmishes & Void Storms
    ~1,500+ Endo per mission plus scrap. Veil Proxima missions drop a ton of Endo naturally, but the real secret is the Railjack Wreckage you acquire. Scrapping unused guns and ship components from your Plexus yields massive amounts of Endo (225 Endo per Tier 3 scrap).
  • Arbitrations (Endgame Survival/Defense)
    Varies by length. Arbitrations heavily drop pure Endo and Ayatan Sculptures in their A, B, and C rotations. Furthermore, you can trade the Vitus Essence you earn at the Arbiters of Hexis for additional rare items or Kuva.

Tier 3: Passive & Weekly Methods

Passive
  • Maroo's Weekly Treasure Hunt
    Visit Maroo at Maroo's Bazaar (Mars) once a week for a guaranteed Ayatan Sculpture. Fill it with Ayatan Stars before dissolving it for maximum Endo output.
  • Daily Sorties
    Completing your 3 daily Sortie missions has a very high chance (28%) of dropping a 4,000 Endo bundle or an Ayatan Anasa Sculpture (worth 3,450 Endo when fully socketed).
  • Dissolving Duplicate Mods
    Go to your Mod Console, filter by "Duplicates," select all of your useless thousands of Vitality/Ammo Drum mods, and click "Dissolve into Endo." You will be shocked by how much Endo you are passively sitting on.

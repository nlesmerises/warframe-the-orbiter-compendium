⚔️ 1. Overview

What Are Arbitrations?

Overview
  • Elite endless missions with significantly increased enemy level and lethality
  • Rotate every hour — mission type and tileset changes on the hour
  • Only one life: if your Warframe dies, you are dead — no revives (unless a teammate revives you)
  • Primary currency: Vitus Essence — used in the Arbitration shop
  • Best passive Endo source in the game at Rotation C+

Access Requirements

Overview
  • All nodes on the star chart must be unlocked (including Dark Sectors and Lua)
  • No Mastery Rank requirement — just full star chart completion
  • Check the Arbitrations terminal in the Relay to see the current mission
  • Available from the Arbitration terminal or Navigation — always active

📋 2. Rules & Mechanics

One Life Rule

Mechanics
  • No self-revives — going down means you spectate unless a squadmate revives you
  • Squadmates can revive downed players using a Resurgence Burden item (appears on death)
  • If no teammates or they choose not to revive — mission over for you
  • Survival skills matter — bring tanky or evasive builds

Boosted Drop Rates

Mechanics
  • Enemies drop Endo in large quantities throughout the mission
  • Vitus Essence drops from enemies randomly — more time = more essence
  • Rotation rewards (C rotation) contain the best items: Ayatan sculptures, Vitus Essence, mods
  • Staying until Rotation C (wave 20 / 20 min) is the efficiency target

Warframe / Weapon Buffs

Mechanics
  • Each Arbitration randomly buffs one Warframe and one weapon type by +300% power
  • Buff shown in the mission selection screen — matching the buff = bonus performance
  • Not mandatory — bring your best survival frame regardless of buff

🖥️ 3. Arbitration Shield Drones

What They Do

Drones
  • Small hovering drones that make nearby enemies completely invulnerable
  • Enemies under drone protection cannot be damaged by any means until the drone is destroyed
  • Drones themselves are fragile — single target damage destroys them quickly
  • The biggest challenge in Arbitrations — ignore drones and you deal zero damage

Countering Drones

Drones
  • Prioritize drones above all other enemies — kill drone first, then the protected unit
  • Weapons with punch-through can hit drones behind cover
  • Warframe abilities that target drones: Gara (Mass Vitrify), Wisp (Motes do not help here — use direct fire)
  • Avoid abilities that deal AoE only — drones require direct targeted damage
  • Lanka, Rubico, Crit primaries excell at fast drone deletion

🤖 4. Recommended Builds

Top Warframe Choices

Builds
  • Revenant — Mesmer Skin provides near-invulnerability, trivializes one-life rule
  • Wisp — Mote buffs + Breach Surge for massive damage; resilient with right build
  • Saryn — Spore spread clears waves efficiently; watch for drones blocking damage
  • Octavia — Mallet + Resonator handles waves; passive invisibility with Metronome
  • Nidus — Cannot die with sufficient stacks; strong for long runs

Weapon Priorities

Builds
  • Bring at least one high single-target weapon for drone deletion
  • Snipers (Lanka, Rubico Prime) excel — fast drone kills + high enemy damage
  • Secondary: any high-damage pistol for backup drone duty
  • Avoid purely AoE-only setups — drones require direct hits

Mod Priorities

Builds
  • Max survivability — Rolling Guard, Brief Respite, Adaptation
  • Energy management — Zenurik school or Arcane Energize if not running Revenant
  • Efficiency matters less than staying alive for long rotations

🔄 5. Rotations & Rewards

Rotation Pattern (AABC)

Rotations
  • Survival: Rotation A = 10 min, A = 20 min, B = 30 min, C = 40 min, then repeats AABC
  • Defense / Interception: similar wave-based rotation
  • Target: stay to at least Rotation C (40 min / ~20 waves) for best Vitus and Endo
  • Beyond C: each subsequent rotation repeats AABC — diminishing new rewards

Reward Highlights

Rotations
  • Endo: drops from every enemy throughout — 1000–3000 per mission at C rotation
  • Vitus Essence: random enemy drops + guaranteed Rotation C reward
  • Ayatan Sculptures: Rotation B & C — fill with stars for bonus Endo
  • Exclusive mods: Aero Vantage, Motus Signal, Swift Momentum, and others only from Arbitration shop

🛒 6. Arbitration Shop

Vitus Essence Purchases

Shop
  • Shop located at the Arbitrations terminal in any Relay
  • Aura Forma — allows any aura polarity on any Warframe slot — high priority
  • Arbitration-exclusive mods — Aero Vantage, Motus Signal, Drift mods, etc.
  • Sculptures — Ayatan Amber Stars for Endo conversion
  • Rotating stock — check weekly for new items

Priority Buy Order

Shop
  • 1. Aura Forma — most impactful purchase; get 1 per major frame build
  • 2. Exclusive mods you are missing — check which ones apply to your builds
  • 3. Ayatan Stars if short on Endo for mod ranking
  • Vitus Essence is not tradeable — spend it, do not hoard

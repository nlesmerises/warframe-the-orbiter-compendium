⚙️ 1. Meta Ship Components (House Brands)

There are three "Houses" that manufacture Railjack parts (Zetki, Vidar, and Lavan). You get MK III parts as random drops from high-level Grineer/Corpus Proxima missions.

🛡️ Plating (Hull/Armor)

Meta: Lavan Plating MK III

Lavan provides the highest raw Health/Armor combination, making your ship incredibly tanky against high-level Grineer fighters.

🔮 Shields

Meta: Zetki Shield Array MK III

Zetki provides the fastest shield recharge delay. In Railjack, having your shields start regenerating instantly is better than having a huge shield pool.

🚀 Engines

Meta: Vidar Engines MK III

Vidar engines give the highest base speed, which is crucial for closing the distance on Crewships and completing missions faster.

⚡ Reactor

Meta: Vidar Reactor MK III

Reactors buff your Battle Mods (Warframe abilities for your ship). Vidar gives the highest Ability Range and Duration bonuses.

👥 2. AI Crew Setup (Ticker in Fortuna)

🤝 The Perfect Solo Crew

Go to Fortuna, visit Ticker, and "Hire Railjack Crew." You want to hire 3 specific crew members based on their stat distributions. Train their stats in the Dry Dock.

  • Crewman 1: The Engineer. Put all their points into Repair and Combat. Equip them with an AOE weapon (Kuva Bramma / Ignis Wraith). If a boarding party attacks, they will kill them. If a fire starts on the ship, they will instantly put it out. You never have to leave the pilot seat.
  • Crewman 2: The Gunner. Put all their points into Gunnery. Assign them to a side turret. They have perfect 360-degree aimbot and will melt fighters you can't even see.
  • Crewman 3: The Second Gunner. Same as Crewman 2. Put points into Gunnery, assign to the other side turret.
🚨 Never assign an AI to be a Pilot. They fly terribly and will ruin your aim. You are the pilot.

⚔️ 3. Railjack Armaments

🔫 Turrets & Ordnance

You need weapons for the Pilot (you) and your AI Gunners.

  • Pilot Turret (Nose): Zetki Photors or Zetki Pulsar. You want hitscan (instant travel time) weapons so you don't have to lead targets. Photors literally burn through Corpus shields instantly.
  • Gunner Turrets (Sides): Zetki Talyn or Zetki Apoc. AI Gunners have perfect tracking, so you can give them projectile weapons and they will never miss.
  • Ordnance (Missiles): Tycho Seeker MK III (Purchased from the Clan Dojo). These are lock-on missiles. Just look at a fighter, press the middle mouse button, and it dies.

💎 4. The Plexus (Railjack Mods)

The Plexus is how you mod your Railjack. It uses Endo and Credits just like Warframe mods.

🃏 Aura & Integrated Mods

  • Aura: Ironclad Matrix (Massive Hull/Armor buff)
  • Hyperstrike: Turret Damage
  • Bulkhead: Railjack Health
  • Hull Weave: Railjack Armor
  • Cruising Speed: Flight Speed
  • Forward Artillery: +Damage to the big laser (Crucial for one-shotting Crewships)
  • Artillery Cheap Shot: 50% chance to not consume Dome Charges when firing the big laser.

⚔️ Battle Mods (The “Abilities”)

These are Warframe abilities, but for your ship. You cast them using 1, 2, and 3.

  • Slot 1: Blackout Pulse (Stuns Crewships)
  • Slot 2: Shatter Burst (Fires a bomb that wipes out groups of fighters)
  • Slot 3: Seeker Volley (Mandatory). The most overpowered ability in Railjack. Press 3, and your ship fires hundreds of homing missiles that instantly kill every fighter within 10,000 meters.

📌 Efficiency Tips

  • Board crewships manually and kill the reactor — faster than shooting them down
  • Tactical Intrinsic allows you to call teammates to objectives from Railjack map
  • Farm Veil Proxima for highest-tier components and Plexus mods
  • Command Rank 10 allows fully crewed solo play — rotate crew to cover all roles
  • Seeker Volley + Void Hole ordnance combo destroys fighter groups instantly

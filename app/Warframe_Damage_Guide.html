🥽 1. Physical Damage Types

🔴 Impact

Physical
  • Status proc: Stagger / Knockback — interrupts enemy attacks
  • +25% damage vs. Shields
  • Strong against: Corpus shields
  • Weak against: Fossilized (Infested)

🟢 Puncture

Physical
  • Status proc: Weakened — target deals 30% less damage
  • +25% damage vs. Armor
  • Strong against: Ferrite Armor (Grineer), Fossilized
  • Weak against: Robotic (Corpus machines)

🟡 Slash

Physical
  • Status proc: Bleed — True damage DoT, ignores armor completely
  • 35% of hit damage × 6 ticks over 6 seconds
  • Strong against: Flesh, Cloned Flesh
  • Best physical type for sustained DPS — pairs perfectly with Viral

🔥 2. Base Elemental Types

🔥 Heat

Elemental
  • Status proc: Burn — DoT + panic animation (enemy stops shooting)
  • Also strips up to 50% armor over multiple procs
  • Strong against: Infested, Cloned Flesh
  • Pairs extremely well with Corrosive

❄️ Cold

Elemental
  • Status proc: Freeze — enemy movement and attack speed -50%
  • +25% vs. Alloy Armor, +25% vs. Shields
  • Strong against: Alloy Armor (Grineer heavies), Corpus shields
  • Weak against: Infested entirely

⚡ Electric

Elemental
  • Status proc: Electrocute — arcs to 3 nearby enemies (AoE stun)
  • +25% vs. Machinery, +25% vs. Robotic
  • Strong against: Corpus robots, MOAs, drones
  • Good crowd control value in dense groups

☣️ Toxin

Elemental
  • Status proc: Poison — DoT that bypasses Shields entirely
  • +50% vs. Flesh, +25% vs. Infested flesh types
  • Strong against: Corpus (ignores shields), Flesh
  • Weak against: Robotic, Infested Sinew

⚗️ 3. Combined Elements

Combine two base elements by equipping their mods together. The in-game Arsenal shows the resulting combination. Order of mods determines which pairs combine first — slot them intentionally.

🌀 Corrosive (Electric + Toxin)

Status: Strips 25% armor per proc — stacks to 80% at 4 procs. Permanent until death.
Best against: Grineer (Ferrite Armor), Corrupted. The backbone of armor-stripping builds.

🦄 Viral (Cold + Toxin)

Status: Reduces target max Health by 50% per stack — stacks to -325% at 6 procs.
Best against: Everything. Combined with Slash bleed (true damage), this is the current dominant meta combination.

🧲 Magnetic (Cold + Electric)

Status: Reduces max Shields by 75% and drains current Shields.
Best against: Corpus shielded units. Essential for high-level Corpus content.

☢️ Radiation (Heat + Electric)

Status: Confusion — enemies attack their own allies for 12 seconds.
Best against: Alloy Armor (Grineer heavies), Sentient cores, Eidolons. Chaos proc is strong CC.

☁️ Gas (Heat + Toxin)

Status: Creates a cloud that procs Toxin on all enemies in range.
Best against: Grouped Infested, dense crowds. AoE poison clouds scale with proc count.

💥 Blast (Heat + Cold)

Status: Knockdown in an area around the target.
Moderate utility — useful for crowd control but outclassed by other combos for raw damage.

📊 4. Faction Weaknesses — Quick Reference

🔴 Grineer

Faction
  • Primary: Corrosive + Heat (strips Ferrite Armor + bonus DoT)
  • Meta: Viral + Slash (bleeds bypass remaining armor)
  • Heavy units with Alloy Armor: Radiation instead of Corrosive
  • Avoid: Magnetic, Blast against armored units

🔵 Corpus

Faction
  • Primary: Magnetic + Toxin (destroys shields, Toxin bypasses them)
  • Robots and MOAs: Corrosive for Robotic health type
  • Pure Toxin secondaries bypass shields entirely on direct hits
  • Avoid: relying solely on Impact (shields regenerate)

🟢 Infested

Faction
  • Primary: Viral + Heat or Corrosive + Heat
  • Gas + Slash = strong AoE clear for dense groups
  • Ancient Healers: Radiation (confusion breaks their aura link)
  • Avoid: Cold, Electric (low effectiveness on Infested flesh)

🟣 Corrupted (Void)

Faction
  • Mixed Grineer and Corpus unit types — varies by enemy
  • Primary: Corrosive + Cold (covers both armor types)
  • Radiation on Alloy-armored Corrupted heavy units
  • Viral + Slash as universal fallback on all unit types

🔬 7. Sentients

📝 Adaptation Mechanic

Sentients
  • Sentients gain 90% resistance to any damage type they are hit with repeatedly
  • Adaptation resets when the Sentient is knocked into a downed state
  • Void damage from Operator Amps permanently breaks their adaptation for a window
  • Always have at least 2–3 damage types on your build to cycle when they adapt

⚡ Eidolon-Specific Strategy

Sentients
  • Eidolon Teralyst/Gantulyst/Hydrolyst have Sentient shields — strip with Operator Void beam first
  • Synovias (glowing joints) exposed only after shield phase — use high-crit weapons
  • Radiation is the primary damage type after shield removal (bonus vs. Alloy Armor body)
  • Chroma’s Vex Armor + Roar, or Volt Shields = standard Eidolon support/DPS setup

🚫 Murmur / Thrax (Zariman)

Sentients
  • Void damage from Operator Amps is highly effective — strips Thrax energy tether
  • Thrax Plasm units must be killed with Void damage or they will revive
  • Standard elementals work on their physical health after tether is broken
  • Radiation recommended for confusion in tight corridors during Void missions

📊 Armor Scaling Reference

Sentients
  • At Level 100: Grineer Lancer has ~11,000 effective HP (accounting for armor)
  • At Level 165 (Steel Path baseline): effective HP scales past 30,000–50,000 range
  • Corrosive (80% strip) + Heat (50% strip) together can reduce armor by 90%+
  • Viral × 3.25 multiplier + armor-stripped target = highest practical damage output
  • Armor formula: EHP = HP × (1 + Armor / 300) — armor past 3,000 yields diminishing returns

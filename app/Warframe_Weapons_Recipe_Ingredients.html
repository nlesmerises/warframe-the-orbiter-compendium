🧪 Foundry Weapon Recipe & Ingredients

Critical Weapons Needed to Craft Other Weapons (Never Sell Until Consumed in the Foundry!)

🛡️ Critical Crafting Ingredients (Keep Until Used)

These weapons are not dead-ends. They serve as physical ingredients required in the Foundry to craft upgraded, twin, or entirely new weapons. Keep these base weapons in your inventory (or waiting in your Foundry) until you have fully consumed them to build their upgrades!

🔫 Primary Recipe Roles

  • Miter Required for: Panthera
  • Grakata Required for: Twin Grakatas
  • Mutalist Cernos Required for: Proboscis Cernos

⚔️ Melee Recipe Roles

  • Dual Skana Required for: Redeemer
  • Kama Required for: Dual Kamas
  • Kogake Required for: Hirudo
  • Ankyros Required for: Tekko
  • Amphis Required for: Sydon

🗡️ Secondary Recipe Roles

  • Furis Required for: Afuris
  • Kunai Required for: Tysis
  • Lato Required for: Bolto & Aklato
  • Bolto Required for: Akbolto
  • Akbolto Required for: Akjagara
  • Kraken Required for: Kulstar
  • Viper Required for: Twin Vipers
  • Gammacor Required for: Synoid Gammacor

📋 1. Essential Mods

🃏 Maxed Corrupted Warframe Mods

You must have Blind Rage, Transient Fortitude, Overextended, Narrow Minded, and Fleeting Expertise farmed from Deimos Vaults and ranked to at least Rank 8.

🃏 Galvanized Weapon Mods

Purchased from the Arbiters of Hexis using Vitus Essence (Arbitrations). You absolutely need Galvanized Chamber (Multishot) and Galvanized Aptitude/Savvy (Status Damage).

🃏 Adaptation / Rolling Guard

Standard survivability mods like Vitality are no longer enough. You need Adaptation (damage resistance stacking) or Rolling Guard (invulnerability clearing) to survive.

✨ 2. Critical Arcanes

🔫 Primary/Secondary Merciless

These drop from Steel Path Acolytes, giving up to +360% Damage and +30% Reload Speed. While you farm them IN the Steel Path, you need to buy or trade for a base set immediately to actually deal damage.

⚡ Energy Economy Solution

Do you have Arcane Energize, Equilibrium + Synth Fiber, or a heavily invested Zenurik Focus school? Running out of energy in Steel Path equals instant death.

🎯 3. Loadout Mechanics

⚗️ 100% Armor Stripping

Grineer armor scales exponentially. You MUST have an ability (like Terrify, Pillage, Tharros Strike, or Unairu focus) that can strip armor, or weapons built for pure Slash/Bleed procs.

🦠 Viral Status Application

A companion (like Panzer Vulpaphyla) or a primer secondary weapon (like Epitaph/Kuva Nukor) built to spread Viral status to multiply damage to health by up to 325%.

🛡️ 4. Survival & Focus

🛡️ Shield Gating Knowledge

Understanding how to use Brief Respite and Catalyzing Shields to instantly reset your shield gate invulnerability window when your shields break.

🔬 Focus School Investment

Having Zenurik's "Wellspring" for energy, or Madurai's "Void Strike" for melting Acolytes and Thrax enemies, fully unlocked and unbound.

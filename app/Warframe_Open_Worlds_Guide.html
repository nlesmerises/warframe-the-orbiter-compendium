🌼 1. Plains of Eidolon — Earth

🏙️ Cetus — Ostron Syndicate

Syndicate
  • Earn Standing via bounties, fishing, and mining
  • Hok's Anvil: Craft Zaw melee weapons (fully modular)
  • Fisher Hai-Luk: Process fish for Ostron resources
  • Old Man Suumbaat: Convert mining gems for standing

🌙 Day/Night Activities

Activities
  • Day: Bounties, Grineer patrols, resource farming, fishing
  • Night: Eidolon hunts (see Eidolon Hunt Guide for full details)
  • Fishing: Murkray (deep water), Norg (night only), Charc Eel
  • Mining: Pyrol, Auron, Coprun — needed for Zaw components

🔮 Quills Syndicate

Syndicate
  • Hidden behind a wall in Cetus — talk to the Quills NPC
  • Earn Standing by trading Sentient Cores from Eidolon fights
  • Higher rank unlocks stronger Amp components (beyond Mote Prism)
  • Essential progression for serious Operator gameplay

🏭 2. Orb Vallis — Venus

🏭 Fortuna — Solaris United

Syndicate
  • Earn Standing by completing Solaris United bounties on the Vallis
  • Rude Zuud: Craft Kitgun secondaries (modular, highly customizable)
  • The Business: Conservation — track and capture animals for standing
  • Smokefinger: Mining equipment and gem exchange

🌊 Key Activities

Activities
  • K-Drive races: Earn Ventkids standing for K-Drive mods and cosmetics
  • Toroids: Vega, Calda, Sola — farm from Corpus bases or Profit-Taker boss
  • Conservation: Pobbers, Bolarolas, Sawgaws — high standing per capture
  • Profit-Taker Orb: Vox Solaris content, Archgun usage required

💼 Vox Solaris — Elite Tier

Elite
  • Unlocked only after reaching max rank with Solaris United
  • Talk to Little Duck in Fortuna's back room (restricted area)
  • Earn via Toroid farming and Profit-Taker bounties
  • Rewards: top-tier Amp components unavailable elsewhere

🧪 3. Cambion Drift — Deimos

🏛️ Necralisk — Entrati Syndicate

Syndicate
  • Daughter: Deimos fishing, Predasite/Vulpaphyla companions
  • Son: Conservation of Deimos creatures for Entrati Standing
  • Father: Necramech parts, Otak tokens, crafting components
  • Loid: Isolation Vault bounties (Cavia-linked content)

🌆 Fass & Vome Cycles

Cycles
  • Fass (day): Ravenous Fass wyrm visible in sky — some fish only spawn here
  • Vome (night): Vome wyrm active — different resource spawns
  • Fass Residue / Vome Residue: unique crafting resources from their corpses
  • Check active cycle before farming specific fish or resources

🏚️ Isolation Vaults

Missions
  • Run bounties from Loid at the bottom floor of the Necralisk
  • Tier 1 → 2 → 3 can be completed in a single run
  • Rewards: Necramech mods, Orokin Matrices, Entrati Standing
  • Required for Necramech progression and Deimos weapon blueprints

🩪 Cavia Syndicate

Syndicate
  • Added with Whispers in the Walls update
  • Earn standing through Deimos bounties and conservation
  • Unlocks Deep Archimedea missions (see Deep Archimedea Guide)
  • Rewards: Grimoire Incarnon upgrades, exclusive Arcanes

⚡ 4. Zariman — Angels of the Zariman

🚀 Chrysalith — Holdfasts Syndicate

Hub
  • Zariman colony survivors — accessed after Angels of the Zariman quest
  • Quinn: Holdfasts bounties and primary lore contact
  • Cavalero: Incarnon Genesis adapters (rotate weekly) and Zariman weapons
  • Earn Voidplume currency from missions and Void entity kills

⚡ Void Mission Types

Missions
  • Void Cascade: Collect Void sparks before Exolizers overfill
  • Void Flood: Seal Void Ruptures spreading across the map
  • Void Armageddon: Survival variant with Void angel encounters
  • Void Survival: Standard survival with Zariman modifiers

🌟 Holdfasts Standing

Standing
  • Earn via completing Zariman missions and Holdfasts bounties from Quinn
  • Voidplume Quills, Dawn, Crest — traded for large standing chunks
  • Drop from Void Angels (boss encounters within missions)
  • Higher rank unlocks more Incarnon adapter offerings from Cavalero

✨ Incarnon Genesis System

System
  • Weapon upgrade adapters that unlock a transformed "Incarnon" fire mode
  • Obtained from Cavalero on a rotating weekly basis (5 weapons offered)
  • Apply to existing weapons — does not consume the base weapon
  • Some of the highest-performing weapon builds in the game use Incarnon adapters

🎯 8. Efficiency Tips by World

💡 Plains of Eidolon

Tips
  • Run Tier 5 bounties for the fastest standing — each stage rewards independently
  • Collect Cetus Wisps during day runs; they respawn each time you enter the Plains
  • Fish at night with bait near ocean water for Murkray — highest Ostron standing fish
  • Bring a Resource Booster if farming fish or mining — doubles yield with no time cost
  • Mining rare gems (Sentirum, Zodian) spawn in cave nodes — prioritize cave sweeps

💡 Orb Vallis

Tips
  • Toroid farming at base camps is the fastest Vox Solaris standing method — use Nekros for extra drops
  • Profit-Taker bounty (Phase 4) drops Crisma Toroids — highest-value Toroid per run
  • Conservation gives more standing per capture than bounties at lower ranks — use Son's Echo-Lures
  • Temperature cycle affects which fish spawn — check before fishing runs
  • K-Drive races near Fortuna give Ventkids standing with no enemy engagement required

💡 Cambion Drift

Tips
  • Chain Isolation Vault tiers (1→2→3) in a single run for maximum Entrati token reward
  • Collect all five NPC token types daily (Son, Daughter, Father, Loid, Mother) for best standing per day
  • Fass/Vome cycle determines which fish and resources are available — plan runs around it
  • Necramech spawns in Vault runs — kill for Necramech mod drops alongside bounty rewards
  • Servofish on Deimos require a Fishing Spear (not a Rod) — buy from Daughter

💡 Zariman

Tips
  • Void Cascade is the most efficient mission type for Voidplume density per run
  • Kill Void Angels for Voidplume Crests — highest standing value per item
  • Check Cavalero's weekly rotation every reset (Monday) — each rotation offers 5 Incarnon adapters
  • Holdfasts bounties from Quinn stack with mission completion standing — always take a bounty before starting
  • Riven Fragments combine in sets of 5 — run multiple missions before claiming to batch your Rivens

⛧️ How to Unlock and Acquire Archon Shards

Unlock: Complete The New War, then complete Veilbreaker. Build and install the Archon Shard Helminth Segment so the Helminth chair can equip shards.
Where to Get Shards: Run the weekly Archon Hunt. Additional shards can come from Netracells and Deep/Elite Archimedea after completing Whispers in the Walls and ranking the Cavia.
Equip or Remove: Use the Helminth chair, choose Archon Shards, select one of five slots, and choose the bonus. Removing a shard costs Bile.
Hybrid Fusion: Buy and install the Coalescent Fusion Segment from Bird 3 in the Sanctum Anatomica. Fuse Crimson, Amber, and Azure combinations with Stela to create Emerald, Topaz, or Violet shards.
Tauforged Fusion: Obtain the Ascent Fusion upgrade from Bird 3 as your Cavia rank allows. It combines three matching normal shards into one Tauforged shard.
You Need: A completed source quest chain, Helminth access, Cavia progression for fusion, Stela for hybrid recipes, and spare shards in your inventory.
Don't have the shard shown in the planner? Crimson, Amber, and Azure are base shard rewards. Emerald requires Azure + Amber, Topaz requires Crimson + Amber, and Violet requires Crimson + Azure through Coalescent Fusion. Tauforged versions come from eligible endgame rewards or from combining three matching normal shards after unlocking Ascent Fusion.

📖 Meta Breakpoints Quick Guide

  • 100% Corrosive Armor Strip: Requires exactly 2 Normal Emerald shards (+4 Max Corrosive Stacks). This pushes Corrosive from 80% to 100% strip.
  • Violet Melee God: Violet Shards grant Melee Crit Damage. The stat is doubled if your Warframe's Max Energy is over 500!
  • The Blind Rage Offset: 5 Normal Crimson (+10% Str each) = +50% Strength. 3 Tauforged Crimson (+15% Str each) = +45% Strength. Use these to offset negative strength mods.
  • Terrify Animation Fix: Nekros' Terrify animation is extremely slow. 1 Tauforged Amber (+37.5% Cast Speed) completely fixes it without wasting a mod slot on Natural Talent.

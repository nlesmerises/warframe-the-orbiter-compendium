🧪 1. Overview

What Is This Area?

Overview
  • Underground laboratory complex beneath the Cambion Drift (Deimos)
  • Introduced in the Whispers in the Walls update
  • Home of the Cavia syndicate — a collective of researchers studying the Infestation and the Void
  • Contains the Sanctum Anatomica — the hub where you interact with Cavia vendors
  • Albrecht Entrati's original research facility; lore-heavy area with strong Void and Infestation themes

Why Come Here?

Overview
  • Farm Incarnon Genesis adapters — the single biggest damage upgrade in the game
  • Complete Isolation Vaults & Arcana bounties for unique rewards
  • Rank up Cavia syndicate for standing rewards and Incarnon Genesis unlocks
  • Collect Voca (story fragments) to progress Albrecht's lore journals
  • Hunt Necraloid decorations and Cavia-specific cosmetics

Vome & Fass Cycles

Cycles
  • Deimos has a two-phase day/night cycle: Vome (day — calmer, yellow sky) and Fass (night — more hostile, orange sky)
  • Cycle is ~2 hours in real time; check the Deimos cycle tracker in-game
  • Certain Isolation Vault bounties and Fishing drops are cycle-gated
  • Fass Residue and Vome Residue are crafting resources dropped only during their respective cycle
  • Residues are used in Necraloid and Cavia component blueprints

🔒 2. Access & Prerequisites

How to Unlock

Access
  • Complete the Whispers in the Walls quest (requires Deimos access and completion of The War Within)
  • Deimos itself requires completion of Heart of Deimos quest
  • Recommended MR: 8+ (higher for Isolation Vault bounties)
  • After the quest, the Sanctum Anatomica hub becomes accessible via the Cambion Drift or directly from Orbiter navigation

Getting There

Access
  • Navigate to Deimos on the star chart
  • Land at Sanctum Anatomica (hub node) or drop into the Cambion Drift and find the underground entrance
  • Inside the Sanctum: speak to Loid, Otak, Fibonacci, Archimedean Yonta, and Tagfer for syndicate content
  • Cavia missions launch from bounty boards inside the Sanctum or from the Drift's surface camps

👥 3. Cavia Syndicate

Syndicate Structure

Syndicate
  • Five Cavia ranks: Stranger → Acquaintance → Associate → Colleague → Partner
  • Standing is earned via Deimos bounties, Isolation Vault completions, and resource donations
  • Daily standing cap scales with MR (same formula as other open world syndicates)
  • No opposing syndicate — no standing loss risk

Key Vendors

Vendors
  • Loid / Otak — primary Cavia vendor; standing gear, blueprints, and cosmetics
  • Fibonacci — Incarnon Genesis adapters (require Partner rank for highest-tier items)
  • Archimedean Yonta — Arcana Isolation Vault bounties and related rewards
  • Tagfer — decorations and Cavia-themed cosmetics

Standing Farming Tips

Farming
  • Isolation Vault bounties give the most standing per run — complete all three tiers back-to-back
  • Surface bounties from the Drift camps fill the cap quickly with low effort
  • Donate excess Deimos resources (Thaumica, Sporulate Sac, etc.) at Loid for bonus standing
  • Bring a Necramech for Vault Tier 3 — heavily recommended
  • Rank up fast: Partner rank unlocks the full Incarnon Genesis catalog at Fibonacci

🎯 4. Mission Types

Surface Bounties (Cambion Drift)

Bounties
  • Standard open world bounty structure — 5 tiers, escalating difficulty
  • Objectives: Exterminate, Capture, Resource Collection, Hive Purge, Caches
  • Rewards: Deimos resources, Orokin Matrix, Necraloid standing, weapon parts
  • Best for: fast standing fill and resource top-ups

Cavia Bounties (Underground)

Bounties
  • Accessed from Sanctum Anatomica — launch directly into underground Labs nodes
  • Standard Exterminate / Defense / Mobile Defense variants inside the lab corridors
  • Higher standing density than surface bounties
  • Rewards include unique Cavia standing tokens and Arcana Vault keys

Netracell (Weekly)

Weekly
  • Weekly endgame mission — locate and crack open a Netracell containment pod
  • 5 attempts per week; each run costs one Pearl (earned from bounties)
  • Enemies are heavily armored and Void-infused — bring high-DPS builds
  • Rewards: Archon Shards (teal/amber/violet), Cavia standing tokens, Oull Requiem mod
  • Teal Archon Shards (ability strength) are uniquely available here — very high value

🚪 5. Isolation Vaults & Arcana

Isolation Vault Tiers

Vaults
  • Three sequential tiers (T1 → T2 → T3) — each entered from the previous vault's exit
  • Objective: deactivate the Infested Defense Mechanism protecting the vault door, then crack the vault
  • Bring a Necramech — mandatory for T2/T3; damage zones inside the vault rooms heavily punish unshielded frames
  • Complete all three in a single run for maximum efficiency

Arcana Bounties

Arcana
  • Post-vault bonus objectives offered by Archimedean Yonta — run immediately after completing T3
  • Types: Capture, Exterminate, Sabotage in the vault aftermath zone
  • Reward pools: Necramech mods, Cortege weapon parts, Mausolon parts, Sporothrix parts, Bonewidow chassis/neuroptics
  • Best source for Necramech mod drops

Vault Rewards

Rewards
  • T1: Necramech components, Cavia standing tokens, Thaumica, Breath of the Eidolon
  • T2: Rare Necramech mods, Damaged Necramech parts (for repair), Sporothrix parts
  • T3: Bonewidow Neuroptics/Chassis, Cortege parts, Mausolon parts, high Cavia standing
  • Weekly bonus chest in T3 — guarantees a rare drop on first clear each week

🔥 6. Incarnon Genesis

What Is Incarnon Genesis?

Overview
  • An adapter that transforms eligible base weapons into Incarnon form — massively boosted stats and alternate fire modes
  • Acquired from Fibonacci in the Sanctum Anatomica using Cavia standing
  • Requires Partner rank with Cavia for most high-tier adapters
  • Once applied (in the Arsenal), the weapon gains a new Evolution path with 5 evolutions unlocked via kill challenges
  • Incarnon form activates by filling the Incarnon Gauge via headshots or kills, then pressing Alt-Fire to transform

Top Priority Adapters

Builds
  • 🔥 Braton — top-tier primary in Incarnon form; reliable and easy to use
  • 🔥 Latron — high single-shot burst; excellent for Steel Path
  • 🔥 Boar — shotgun with absurd AoE in Incarnon form
  • 🔥 Lex — pistol powerhouse; pairs with dual Lex builds
  • 🔥 Torid — poison cloud primary; top pick for endgame
  • 🔥 Furax — melee Incarnon; strong slam attack synergy
  • 🔥 Dual Ichor — meta melee with high slash proc output
  • 🔥 Ceramic Dagger — extreme crit/status combo in Incarnon form

Rotation & Weekly Unlock

Rotation
  • Fibonacci offers a rotating weekly selection of adapters — not all are available every week
  • Rotation resets weekly on Wednesday (same as Nightwave, Archon Hunt)
  • Check the current week's rotation on the Warframe wiki or community sites
  • You can only install one Incarnon Genesis adapter per weapon — choose wisely
  • Adapters are tradeable between players on the market
  • Evolving the weapon fully requires running Void Cascade or Netracell for Pathos Clamps (weekly cap)

Evolution Challenges

Evolution
  • Each weapon has 5 evolution tiers unlocked by completing in-mission challenges (kills, headshots, slam kills, etc.)
  • Each evolution tier offers a passive bonus perk — choose one of two options per tier
  • Final evolution (Tier 5) gives the most impactful perk — prioritize unlocking this
  • Challenges can be completed in any mission; no special location required
  • Fully evolved Incarnon weapons rival or exceed most Rivens in raw output

🏆 7. Key Rewards

Exclusive Weapons

Weapons
  • Cortege — Necramech heavy weapon; rapid-fire projectile launcher
  • Mausolon — Necramech primary; top auto rifle with innate Void damage
  • Sporothrix — secondary crossbow; high toxin/slash output, excellent for status builds
  • Cedo — primary shotgun; builds status stacks from alt-fire then detonates with primary; top-tier status weapon
  • All require parts from Isolation Vault and Cambion Drift resource drops

Unique Mods

Mods
  • Necramech mods — Mech-specific power, armor, and ability mods; primarily from Arcana bounties
  • Molt Augmented / Molt Reconstruct / Molt Vigor — Arcane-grade mods with very strong passive effects; obtained from Cavia standing
  • Melee Influence — top-tier melee arcane; one of the strongest electrical-proc multipliers in the game
  • Primary Blight / Secondary Blight — strong corrosive proc mods from high-level lab bounties

Arcanes

Arcanes
  • Arcane Avenger — flat crit chance on taking damage; stacks to +45% crit chance; extremely potent
  • Arcane Energize — energy restore on energy pickup; core for energy-hungry builds
  • Arcane Guardian — flat armor bonus on taking damage; tanking staple
  • All three obtainable from Deimos bounty reward pools and Vault completions
  • Rank arcanes to R5 by combining five copies

Crafting Resources

Resources
  • Thaumica — rare Deimos mineral; required for Necramech component blueprints
  • Fass Residue / Vome Residue — cycle-specific drops; used in multiple Cavia blueprints
  • Sporulate Sac — uncommon infested drop; standing donation item
  • Pathos Clamp — weekly-capped resource used to unlock Incarnon Evolution tiers (obtained from Netracell)
  • Orokin Matrix — used in Necramech crafting and Cavia offerings

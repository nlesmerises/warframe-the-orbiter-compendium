🔄 How to Unlock and Install Incarnon Genesis

Unlock Duviri: Complete The Duviri Paradox so the Circuit is available from the Duviri navigation screen.
Unlock Steel Path: Complete the required Star Chart nodes, speak with Teshin, and switch the Circuit to Steel Path difficulty.
Select Rewards: At the beginning of each weekly rotation, choose two of the five offered Incarnon Genesis adapters. They are earned through the Steel Path Circuit reward track.
Earn the Adapters: Play successive Circuit stages and reach the required reward tiers. The selected adapters are awarded separately as you progress.
Install at Cavalero: Own the compatible base weapon, visit Cavalero in the Chrysalith, select Evolve Incarnon Weapons, and provide the adapter plus the listed Duviri resources.
Finish Evolutions: Equip the weapon and complete each displayed evolution challenge. Return to the Incarnon menu to choose the unlocked perks.

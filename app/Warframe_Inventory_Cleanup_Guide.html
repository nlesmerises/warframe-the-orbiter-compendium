📦 Inventory & Space Optimizer

Complete checklists & safeguards for managing slots, mod duplicates, and blueprints

Slot pressure hits every Tenno eventually. This optimizer is a set of safe cleanup checklists — what you can dump for Credits, Endo, and Ducats, and just as importantly what you must never sell. Pick a card below; each opens its own checklist.

Golden rule: when in doubt, don't sell it. Most gear is cheap to keep but painful to refarm — sell only what's on a Hit-List, never what's on the Safeguard list.

🌿 The Helminth Safeguard

Crucial Rule

NEVER sell non-prime Warframes for Credits!

Even if you get their superior Prime version, keep the regular Warframe in your inventory (or keep it waiting in your Foundry) until you unlock the Helminth System in the late-game.

You will "subsume" (feed) these regular Warframes to your ship to permanently unlock their signature ability (like Rhino's Roar or Mirage's Eclipse) so you can cast them on any other warframe.

Selling regular frames for Credits now to save slots means you will have to farm them all over again in the future!

📌 Keep-One Rules

  • Subsume once The ability unlocks account-wide; you don't need the frame afterward.
  • Keep one of each Hold a single non-Prime copy until subsumed; extras can go.
  • Costs resources + time Subsuming takes materials and a day timer — bank mats first.
  • Primes can't subsume Use the regular version to feed Helminth.

🌿 Most-Wanted Subsume Abilities

  • Roar (Rhino) Universal damage buff.
  • Eclipse (Mirage) Big damage / damage reduction.
  • Nourish (Grendel) Viral damage + energy economy.
  • Gloom (Sevagoth) Enemy slow + lifesteal.
  • Pillage (Hildryn) Shield + armor strip.

✅ Housekeeping Checklist

Credits & Endo

1. The Blueprint Credit Goldmine:
Go to Inventory -> Miscellaneous. You naturally accumulate hundreds of spare blueprints from boss farms and standard play (such as Harrow Chassis or Oberon parts). You can sell these duplicates for 10,000+ Credits each. Clear them out for hundreds of thousands of free Credits instantly without touching active gear slots!

2. Safe-Dissolve Duplicate Mods for Endo:
In your Mod Console, filter by Duplicates. When selecting your common card stacks (like Redirection or Vitality), choose the "Select All But One" option (using middle-mouse button or console command). This safely melts down your extra 50 copies into pure Endo, while protecting your baseline upgrade cards!

  • 3. Spare Prime parts Convert duplicates to Ducats — see the Prime Parts → Ducats card.
  • 4. Claim/clear the Foundry Claim finished items and delete stale in-progress builds you'll never finish.
  • 5. Deploy idle Extractors Run Titan/Distilling extractors on cleared planets instead of letting them sit in inventory.
  • 6. Fish, gems & ore Sell raw stock you've already used for standing; keep refined only if a recipe needs it.
  • 7. Decorations & Captura These don't use gear slots — never sell them to "save space."

🎴 Prime Parts → Ducats

Baro Currency

Duplicate Prime parts are the #1 source of Foundry clutter. Instead of hoarding them, turn them into Ducats at any relay's Ducat Kiosk (beside Baro Ki'Teer's spot) to spend on Baro's rotating stock.

🔄 What to Convert

  • Duplicate Prime parts You already own / already built the full set.
  • Common (bronze) parts Pile up fastest — dump freely.
  • Vaulted sets you won't build Convert rather than store indefinitely.

💱 Ducat Value & Tips

  • Common = 15 Ducats Convert in bulk.
  • Silver = 45 / Gold = 100 Highest value — but check plat value first; some sell well.
  • Keep one full set Of anything you actually use; convert the rest.
  • Stockpile before Baro He visits a relay every 2 weeks.

Check plat value first: Trading Ledger · Relics Database · Vendor Tracking

Useless & Safe-to-Dissolve Mods

Free Up Endo!

Melt these mods down in your Mod Console for pure Endo without a second thought. They are either obsolete tutorials, useless stats, or easily replaced duplicates. Keep exactly one maxed-out copy of any useful mods, and safely dissolve the rest!

Defective & Obsolete Resistance Mods

  • Damaged / Defective Mods Tutorial versions. Outclassed completely. Dissolve all!
  • Warm Coat Useless shield resistance. Outclassed.
  • Lightning Rod Electricity resistance. Never used in meta.
  • Flame Repellent Heat resistance. Completely redundant.
  • Antitoxin Toxin resistance. Outclassed by Shield Gating.
  • Insulated Shell Cold resistance. Pure junk.
  • Diamond Skin Radiation resistance. Useless utility.

Obsolete Utility & Sentinel Mod Stacks

  • Intruder Extra hacking seconds. Completely bypassed by Ciphers.
  • Provocation Obsolete bleeding state damage boost. Useless.
  • Vacuum / Fetch Duplicates Keep exactly 1 maxed copy. Dissolve all duplicate stacks.
  • Assault Mode / Striker Duplicates Sentinel action triggers. Melt extra copies.
  • Thief's Wit / Loot Detector Duplicates Keep 1 copy for builds. Dissolve duplicates.
  • Ammo Drum Duplicates Melt down stacks of 100+ copies of common ammo/clip mods.

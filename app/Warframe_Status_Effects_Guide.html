📋 1. How Procs Work

Status Chance & Procs

Overview
  • Status Chance: probability that each hit applies a status effect (proc)
  • Each proc applies an effect based on the damage type that triggered it
  • Multiple procs of the same type stack in count (not magnitude) — up to 10 stacks
  • Procs have individual durations — they expire independently
  • Some procs (Heat, Cold, Toxin) stack magnitude — details per type below

Status vs Crit Builds

Overview
  • High status chance (60%+) enables reliable proc application each shot burst
  • Shotguns calculate status per pellet — multishot increases total procs per trigger
  • Most endgame builds combine both: status procs to debuff, crits for raw damage
  • Viral + Slash is the dominant meta combo — see Meta Combos section

🔳 2. Physical Procs

🔳 Impact

Physical
  • Effect: Staggers the enemy briefly; at 4+ stacks — applies Knockdown
  • Also increases the vulnerability window for other damage types
  • Good for disrupting enemies and enabling follow-up hits
  • Strong against: Shields (Corpus)

✂️ Puncture

Physical
  • Effect: Reduces enemy damage output by 30% per stack (up to 10 stacks = 100% reduction)
  • Very useful for survivability against high-damage enemies
  • Strong against: Armor (Grineer Ferrite Armor)

🟡 Slash

Physical
  • Effect: Applies a Bleed DoT dealing 35% of base damage per tick, 6 ticks over 6 seconds
  • Bleed damage bypasses armor entirely — true damage
  • Stacks multiply: 10 Slash stacks = 10 simultaneous Bleed DoTs running
  • Combined with Viral (max HP reduction): Slash DoT becomes devastating
  • Best physical damage type for endgame DPS against all enemy types

🔥 3. Elemental Procs

🔥 Heat

Elemental
  • Effect: Applies a Burn DoT; stacks increase duration and damage of the burn
  • Also strips up to 50% of enemy armor at max stacks — very powerful
  • Heat strip is a major mechanic: 6 Heat procs reduce armor by 50% permanently until end of mission
  • Works alongside Corrosive for full armor strip combinations
  • Strong against: Infested, Flesh

❄️ Cold

Elemental
  • Effect: Slows enemy movement and attack speed; at 4 stacks — Freeze (brief stun)
  • Also increases enemy susceptibility to Frost abilities
  • Strong against: Shields (massive bonus vs Corpus shields)
  • Useful in Void Fissure corrupted missions where enemies are shield-heavy

⚡ Electricity

Elemental
  • Effect: Chain Lightning that arcs to nearby enemies; also stuns briefly
  • Chain reaches up to 5 meters — excellent in dense enemy groups
  • Strong against: Machinery (Corpus robots), Shields
  • Combined with Toxin → Viral; combined with Cold → Magnetic

☣️ Toxin

Elemental
  • Effect: Applies a Poison DoT that bypasses Shields entirely — hits HP directly
  • Stacks increase Poison damage — extremely powerful against Corpus shields
  • Combined with Cold → Viral; combined with Heat → Gas
  • Strong against: Flesh (Grineer/Infested), Corpus (shields don’t block it)

⚙️ 4. Combined Elements

💀 Viral (Cold + Toxin)

Combined
  • Effect: Reduces enemy maximum HP by up to 50% at 1 stack; stacks to 325% multiplier at 10
  • Does not deal direct damage — amplifies ALL subsequent damage taken by the enemy
  • Pairs devastatingly with Slash DoT: Viral halves HP, Slash bleeds through armor
  • The single most powerful status combination for general content
  • Strong against: Flesh (Grineer), Infested

🔥 Corrosive (Electricity + Toxin)

Combined
  • Effect: Permanently reduces enemy armor by 26% per stack; 10 stacks = max ~80% armor removal
  • Best for fighting heavily armored enemies (Grineer Ferrite/Alloy Armor, Steel Path)
  • Combine with Heat for near-full armor strip (Corrosive 80% + Heat 50% — effectively 100%)
  • Strong against: Ferrite Armor (Grineer), Infested Sinew

🔥 Blast (Heat + Cold)

Combined
  • Effect: Knockdown on proc; disarms enemies at higher stacks
  • Disarm removes enemy weapons — useful in high-density defense missions
  • Less damage-focused than Viral or Corrosive
  • Strong against: Machinery, Robotic

🧠 Magnetic (Cold + Electricity)

Combined
  • Effect: Reduces enemy Shield capacity by up to 75% per stack; disables energy at 4+ stacks
  • Best against Corpus shield-heavy enemies and Nullifier bubbles
  • Energy drain on proc can disable Warframe abilities — avoid in self-AoE scenarios
  • Strong against: Shields (Corpus), Proto Shields

☢️ Gas (Heat + Toxin)

Combined
  • Effect: Releases a toxic cloud around the enemy dealing AoE Toxin DoT to nearby units
  • Excellent crowd-clear proc — one proc spreads Poison to a cluster
  • Strong against: Infested, Flesh, dense enemy groups
  • Synergizes well with melee builds in close-quarters combat

☢️ Radiation (Heat + Electricity)

Combined
  • Effect: Makes the enemy Confused — they attack their own allies
  • Extremely useful in Eidolon fights (Lure confusion) and Steel Path crowd management
  • Confused enemies deal full damage to allies — effective against group enemies
  • Strong against: Alloy Armor (Grineer), Robotic (Sentients)

🏆 5. Meta Combinations

Viral + Slash (Universal DPS)

Meta Build
  • Apply Viral first (reduces max HP), then stack Slash procs (armor-bypassing bleed)
  • Works on every enemy type — the dominant endgame combo
  • Best weapons: those with innate Slash + high status chance; add Viral mods
  • Mods: Primed Pressure Point + Condition Overload for melee; Serration + Viral mods for guns

Corrosive + Heat (Heavy Armor Strip)

Meta Build
  • Use against Steel Path Grineer and Armored Corpus
  • Corrosive strips ~80% armor; Heat strips additional 50% — effectively full strip
  • After strip: all damage types deal full value — switch to high damage weapon
  • Best against: Nox, Bombard, Heavy Gunner at high Steel Path levels

Magnetic + Toxin (Corpus Counter)

Meta Build
  • Magnetic destroys shields; Toxin bypasses remaining shields hitting HP
  • Or use Viral + Toxin for HP reduction + direct HP damage
  • Best in Corpus-heavy missions (Spy, Disruption, Railjack Corpus)

🔧 6. Enemy Type Matchups

Grineer

Enemy Types
  • Heavy armor: use Corrosive + Heat for full strip
  • Flesh under armor: Viral + Slash after armor is stripped
  • Avoid: Radiation (weak bonus vs Grineer), pure Impact

Corpus

Enemy Types
  • Shields: use Magnetic or Cold to strip shields fast
  • Flesh: Viral + Slash once shields are down
  • Robotic units: Radiation + Electricity
  • Avoid: Toxin bypasses shields but less effective than Magnetic for total strip

Infested

Enemy Types
  • Flesh: Viral + Slash or Gas for crowd control
  • Sinew (Ancient Healer): Corrosive
  • Avoid: Cold (Infested resistant); Magnetic (no shields to strip)

Sentients (Eidolons, Zariman)

Enemy Types
  • Adapt to damage types — they resist the last type used after a few hits
  • Void damage (Operator Amp) always effective — no resistance
  • Radiation deals bonus damage to Sentient alloy
  • Rotate damage types mid-fight if resistance triggers

♻️ 1. Disposal Protocols

♻️ Weapon Disposal Protocol

Weapons

Follow this step-by-step ruleset to free up inventory weapon slots without accidentally selling required items or losing Mastery XP.

  • 1Verify Mastery Level 30
    Look at the weapon in your Inventory or Arsenal. Ensure the green "Rank 30" label with the green/gold Mastery Symbol is visible.
    CRITICAL: Never sell a weapon under Level 30! You lose the Master Points forever!
  • 2Cross-Reference the "Hit-List"
    Open your Inventory Space Optimizer (Hit-List). Ensure the weapon is explicitly listed there as a "Safe-to-Discard" dead-end weapon.
    CRITICAL: If it is NOT on the Hit-List, do not sell it!
  • 3Check Foundry Recipe Ingredients
    Quickly search the weapon in the Foundry Recipes & Ingredients sheet. Confirm it is not needed as a physical component to build something else (like Miter or Lato).
  • 4Navigate to Inventory & Sell
    Press Esc -> Equipment -> Inventory. Click the Weapons tab. Click the weapon, check the check-box, and hit "Sell Selected" in the bottom-right corner.

🏃 Mod Dissolving Protocol

Mods

Follow this step-by-step process to safely clear mod clutter and convert thousands of duplicate copies into pure upgrade Endo.

  • 1Open Mod Console
    Interact with the Mod Console (located down the ramp of your Orbiter on the front left side).
  • 2Filter by "Duplicates"
    Click Sort by: Duplicates at the top right of the mod console. This clusters your duplicate stacks together, hiding single upgraded cards.
  • 3Execute "Select All But One"
    When clicking a duplicate stack, select the "Select All But One" shortcut (e.g. click middle-mouse button or click the dedicated UI button).
    CRITICAL: This ensures you keep exactly 1 copy to use or upgrade in the future!
  • 4Dissolve into Endo
    Once all duplicates are selected, click "Dissolve" at the top left of the screen to convert the cards into pure Endo.
    CRITICAL: Never choose "Sell for Credits"! Credits are easily farmed, but Endo is rare!

✅ 3. Pre-Flight Tenno Clean-Up Checklist

Run through these check-boxes before executing any inventory purging sessions in-game!

  • Is the weapon fully Rank 30? (Verified in Arsenal)
  • Is it listed on the Safe-to-Discard Hit-List?
  • Did I make sure it has no Helminth or recipe roles?
  • Did I filter my Mod Console by "Duplicates"?
  • Did I select "Select All But One" to save my baseline?
  • Am I dissolving duplicates for Endo (NOT selling for Credits)?

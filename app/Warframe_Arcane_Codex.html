✨ 1. What Are Arcanes?

⚡ Conditional Power Boosts

  • Arcanes are equippable enhancements that trigger on a condition (on hit, on kill, on headshot, on taking damage, on ability cast).
  • Most have a chance to proc and a duration, and many stack (e.g. Energize, Merciless) up to a cap.
  • They are separate from mods — arcanes occupy dedicated arcane slots, so they add power without costing mod capacity.
  • Arcanes are a mid-to-late game power layer. You do not need them to clear the star chart; they shine in Steel Path and endurance content.

📑 Rank 0–5 & the 21-Copy Rule

  • Most arcanes rank from 0 to 5. Higher rank = stronger effect and/or higher proc chance.
  • You rank up by combining identical copies. Reaching Rank 5 needs 21 total copies of that arcane.
  • A handful of Amp and Kitgun arcanes cap their effect at fewer copies (10 total).
  • Owning duplicates beyond a maxed arcane = pure trade/sell value.

🧩 3. Arcane Slots — Where They Go

🧠 Warframe (×2)

  • Every Warframe has two arcane slots, unlocked once.
  • Unlock by ranking any Warframe arcane to a threshold once, or via the in-game prompt — after that all frames share the slots.
  • This is the highest-impact pair: survivability + energy or DPS.

🗡 Weapons (×1 each)

  • Primary, Secondary, and Melee each have one arcane slot.
  • Weapon arcanes scale damage hard in endgame (Merciless line, Melee Arcanes).

👀 Operator & Amp

  • Operator suit takes Magus arcanes; Amps take Virtuos arcanes.
  • Critical for Eidolon hunting and survivability during Operator play.

🐾 Companions

  • Robotic companions / Sentinels use companion arcanes (e.g. survivability, looter utility).
  • Lower priority than frame and weapon arcanes for most builds.

⚔️ 5. Weapon & Melee Arcanes

🔫 Primary / Secondary

  • Primary/Secondary Merciless — on kill: stacking damage (up to 12×), the top DPS arcane for most guns.
  • Primary/Secondary Deadhead — on headshot kill: damage + bonus headshot multiplier.
  • Primary/Secondary Dexterity — on kill: damage that also feeds melee.
  • Source: Steel Path Incursions / Cavalero (Zariman) standing.

⚔️ Melee Arcanes

  • Melee has its own arcane line (e.g. Melee Influence, Melee Vortex, Melee Crescendo, Melee Animosity, Melee Exposure).
  • Influence (electric spread) and Crescendo (stacking combo-based damage) are standout picks.
  • Source: Steel Path Circuit (Duviri) weekly rewards — see the Circuit Tracker.

👁️ 6. Operator (Magus) & Amp (Virtuos) Arcanes

✨ Magus (Operator suit)

Magus Lockdown (CC on void dash), Magus Elevate (heal on transference), Magus Repair (heal allies), Magus Husk (armor), Magus Cadence (sprint). Elevate + Lockdown is the staple Eidolon/utility pair.

✨ Virtuos (Amp)

Virtuos Strike (amp crit), Virtuos Shadow (void-status crit), Virtuos Forge (Unairu synergy). Essential damage boosts for Eidolon and Profit-Taker amp play — see the Eidolon Hunt Guide.

🪙 8. Ranking Priority & Plat Strategy

⭐ Max These First

  • Survivability first: a maxed Grace, Guardian, or Blessing keeps you alive in Steel Path far more than a half-ranked DPS arcane.
  • Then economy: Energize unlocks ability-heavy frames.
  • Then damage: Merciless on your main gun, Melee Influence/Crescendo on your main melee.

💰 Buy vs. Farm

  • Standing-bought arcanes (Zariman, Cavia) are guaranteed — cheaper than farming RNG for newer players.
  • High-value Eidolon arcanes are often cheaper to trade for than to farm if you dislike Tridolons.
  • Reaching Rank 5 = 21 copies; partial ranks (R3–R4) capture most of the benefit if copies are scarce.

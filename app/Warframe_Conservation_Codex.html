🦋 1. What Is Conservation?

Catch, Don't Kill

Overview
  • Conservation is non-lethal hunting: you tranquilize wild animals and capture them for Standing, species tags, and Floofs — no killing.
  • Available on all three open worlds: Orb Vallis, Plains of Eidolon, and Cambion Drift.
  • It's a relaxed, low-pressure activity — a steady standing/decoration source.

Why Do It

Overview
  • Syndicate Standing from turn-ins (Solaris United / Ostron / Entrati).
  • Tags → redeem for Floofs (collectible Orbiter decorations).
  • Some rare resources and the satisfaction of a perfect capture streak.

🎯 2. How a Hunt Works

🔄 The Loop

  • Find a track (footprints) or use an Echo-Lure to call the species.
  • Mimic the lure tones when prompted to coax the animal in (a small rhythm minigame).
  • Wait for it to appear, then tranquilize it with the Tranq Rifle — do not use a real weapon.
  • Capture it. A clean, undisturbed approach yields a Perfect Capture.

🏆 Perfect Captures

Tranquilizing without spooking the animal (no gunfire nearby, no missed darts) grants a Perfect Capture, which gives the best rewards and more tags. Approach crouched, keep Warframe powers/allies from scaring them, and tag every animal in a group before they flee.

🎁 5. Rewards

Tags & Floofs

Rewards
  • Each successful turn-in grants Standing and a species tag.
  • Trade tags back to the vendor for Floofs — collectible Orbiter decorations.
  • Perfect captures yield more tags per run.

Standing & Resources

Rewards
  • Solid daily standing toward the world's syndicate — see Syndicate Manager.
  • Some animals tie into resources/companions for that world.
  • Great to combine with bounties and fishing on the same trip.

💡 6. Tips

  • Never shoot with a real weapon — only the Tranq Rifle; a kill ruins the capture.
  • Use the right Echo-Lure for the species you want, and a Pheromone item when farming rares.
  • Go solo or with a coordinated squad — random gunfire and abilities scare animals.
  • Approach crouched and tranq from range for clean Perfect Captures.
  • Pair the trip with open-world bounties & fishing — see the Open Worlds Guide and Fishing Codex.

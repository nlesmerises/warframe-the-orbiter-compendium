📜 1. How Quests Work

🧭 Finding & Starting

  • Quests live in your Codex → Quests. Select one to set it active.
  • Many are gated behind star-chart progress, junctions, or completing an earlier quest.
  • Some unlock from relays / open-world syndicates (e.g. Vox Solaris, Saya's Vigil).

🏆 Why They Matter

  • Main quests unlock core systems (Operator, Focus, Railjack, open worlds).
  • Side quests are the main way to unlock many Warframes for free.
  • Story progress gates endgame: The New War is a prerequisite for several later quests and systems.

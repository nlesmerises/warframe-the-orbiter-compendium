The Pennant Two-Handed Nikana grants a +100% Melee Attack Speed buff on Heavy Kill. Heavy attacks with Two-Handed Nikanas have guaranteed Slash procs (bypassing armor).

Using Initial Combo locks you at 2x Combo so every heavy attack deals double damage instantly:

  • Critical Setup: Sacrificial Steel (+440% CC on heavy attacks)

  • Heavy Wind-up Speed: Killing Blow (+120% Damage, +60% Wind-up Speed)

⚔️ Pennant: Hybrid Tennokai Build

Endgame

If you have access to the Melee Exilus slot and Tennokai mods, use this 12x combo scaling configuration:

  • Exilus/Tennokai: Dreamer's Wrath or Discipline's Merit (Free full-damage heavy attacks)
  • Combo Modding: Blood Rush (Up to +440% CC at 12x), Weeping Wounds (Up to +440% SC)
  • Base Damage Scaling: Condition Overload (+80% Melee Damage per status)
  • Primer Strategy: Shoot groups with a Kuva Nukor or Epitaph before hitting Tennokai to deal millions of damage.

📈 Ventkids Standing Route

Progression

Standing is the real gate on K-Drive progression. Build one board early, then chain races and tricks in Orb Vallis until you unlock the full modular catalog.

  • Starter standing: complete nearby Orb Vallis races from Fortuna elevator launch points.
  • Fast fallback: trick-combo on coolant towers, pipes, and Fortuna rooftops between race resets.
  • Why craft one early: equipping any custom K-Drive unlocks K-Drive mod usage and Yareli progression steps.
  • Rank gates: higher Ventkids ranks open the better board parts and cosmetics, not just style variants.

Open World

Deimos resources gate Necramechs, Predasites/Vulpaphylas, and other late systems. Refine before you are desperate for them.

💣 Fomorian Disruptor

Event Craft

The Fomorian Disruptor is crafted during Balor Fomorian events to allow the Archwing sabotage mission. Keep this here because it is easy to forget the event-only prerequisite.

  • Blueprint source: event inbox / event state when the Balor Fomorian returns.
  • Main requirement: event-only resources collected from Invasion-style or event nodes.
  • Use case: needed to enter the sabotage mission and claim event rewards before the window closes.

🔐 Razorback Cipher

Event Craft

Razorback events use a crafted cipher as the mission-entry key. Build them in batches so the event timer does not force a last-minute resource scramble.

  • Blueprint source: market/event activation when Razorback Armada appears.
  • Why it matters: the key is consumed on entry, so multiple runs require multiple ciphers.
  • Prep note: keep common crafting resources available because the event cadence is irregular.

💎 1. How Relics Work

The Relic System

Overview
  • Relics are consumable items containing 6 possible rewards (3 common, 2 uncommon, 1 rare)
  • Run a Void Fissure mission with a relic equipped — complete the mission to crack it
  • Collect 10 Reactant from Corrupted enemies during the mission to crack the relic
  • In squads: all 4 players’ relics crack simultaneously — choose one reward from the pool of 4
  • Squad sharing multiplies your effective reward pool — always run with a squad when targeting rare items

Rarity Breakdown

Drop Rates
  • Common (Bronze) — 3 items, 25.33% chance each at Intact
  • Uncommon (Silver) — 2 items, 11% chance each at Intact
  • Rare (Gold) — 1 item, 2% chance at Intact; up to 10% at Radiant
  • Refinement increases rare drop chance significantly — always refine before targeting a rare

📅 2. Relic Eras & Tiers

Relic Eras

Tiers
  • Axi — highest level; newest and most valuable Prime parts; run in level 40+ fissures
  • Neo — mid-tier; good mix of current and recent parts; run in level 30–40 fissures
  • Meso — older parts; useful for Ducat farming or completing older Prime sets; run in level 20–30 fissures
  • Lith — lowest era; oldest parts; primarily Ducat value; run in level 1–20 fissures

Fissure Mission Levels

Missions
  • Lith: Capture/Exterminate at low level — fastest missions available
  • Meso: Defense wave 5 or Capture at mid-level
  • Neo: Interception or Defense wave 5 at higher levels
  • Axi: Survival 5 min or Defense wave 5 at level 40+
  • Requiem: special tier for Lich/Sister weapon parts — requires Requiem Relics

💡 3. Refinement

Refinement Levels

Mechanics
  • Intact — base state; 2% rare chance; costs 0 Void Traces
  • Exceptional — 4% rare chance; costs 25 Void Traces
  • Flawless — 6% rare chance; costs 50 Void Traces
  • Radiant — 10% rare chance; costs 100 Void Traces

When to Refine

Strategy
  • Always refine to Radiant when specifically targeting the rare drop
  • Run Intact relics when farming common/uncommon items or Ducat fodder — save Traces
  • Squad of 4 Radiants = 4× 10% chances to get the rare in a single mission
  • Never refine relics you plan to sell or that contain only low-value items

🎯 4. Fissure Mission Selection

Fastest Mission Types

Speed
  • Capture — fastest; complete in 90 seconds; best for Lith/Meso
  • Exterminate — 2–3 minutes; reliable Reactant collection
  • Defense (Wave 5 extract) — 5 waves then extract immediately; good for all tiers
  • Survival (5 min extract) — easy Reactant collection; standard for Axi
  • Avoid: Interception, Mobile Defense — slower Reactant collection

Reactant Tips

Mechanics
  • Corrupted enemies (yellow glowing) drop Reactant — 10 needed to crack the relic
  • Do not rush ahead of Corrupted spawns — they need to reach you before you kill them
  • In a squad: Reactant is per-player — each player must collect 10 individually
  • Capture missions: complete the capture first, then kill Corrupted for Reactant

📈 5. Which Relics to Run

High-Value Target Relics

Priority
  • Check Warframe.market for current Prime part prices — target relics with 50p+ rare items
  • Newly vaulted Prime items spike in value immediately — crack their relics before they vault
  • Newly unvaulted Prime items are cheapest during their unvault window — buy parts then
  • Use the Warframe wiki Void Relic pages to see which relics contain specific parts

Relic Rotation Strategy

Strategy
  • Focus on 1–2 target relics per session — spreading across many relics is inefficient
  • Stock up on Radiants before a group session — refine in bulk at the Void Relic Refinement station
  • Run Intact relics for Ducat farming — no Trace cost
  • Sell surplus Prime parts on Warframe.market rather than converting to Ducats when prices are high

Vaulted vs. Unvaulted

Vault
  • Vaulted — relics no longer in the drop pool; parts only obtainable via trading
  • Unvaulted — temporarily returned to drop pool via Unvaulting event; farm during this window
  • Track the vault cycle on the Warframe wiki for upcoming returns
  • Vaulted parts command higher trade prices — check market before discarding

💰 6. Ducat Value

Ducat Tiers

Value
  • Common parts — 15 Ducats each at Baro Ki’Teer kiosk
  • Uncommon parts — 45 Ducats each
  • Rare parts — 100 Ducats each
  • Ducats are used exclusively with Baro Ki’Teer for exclusive mods, weapons, and cosmetics

When to Convert vs. Sell

Strategy
  • Convert to Ducats when part’s market value is below 10 Platinum — not worth listing
  • Sell on Warframe.market when part value exceeds 15–20 Platinum
  • Never convert rare parts to Ducats unless you have surplus — 100 Ducats ≈ 10–15p at best
  • Build a Ducat reserve of 2000–3000 before Baro visits to cover top-tier purchases

🔸 7. Void Traces

Earning Traces

Farming
  • Earned by cracking relics — base amount plus bonus from any active Void Trace booster
  • Cap: 3600 Void Traces maximum — stop earning when capped
  • Farming Intact relics via fast Capture missions is the most efficient Trace/hour method

Managing Your Reserve

Strategy
  • Keep 500–1000 Traces in reserve for rapid Radiant refinement when a valuable relic appears
  • Do not hoard above 2000 — you lose potential Traces earned from missions
  • Refine in bulk before squad sessions to maximize efficiency
  • Void Trace boosters double traces earned per relic crack — use during active farming

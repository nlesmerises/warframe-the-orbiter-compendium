📚 1. Overview

What Are Orb Mothers?

Overview
  • Two massive boss fights on the Orb Vallis (Venus open world): Profit-Taker Orb and Exploiter Orb
  • Profit-Taker is a multi-phase fight requiring Archgun use, Eidolon-style shield cycling, and specific element matching
  • Exploiter Orb is a two-phase fight using thermia canisters — unique mechanic involving terrain hazards and coolant vents
  • Both drop rare Corpus loot, crafting materials, and standalone mods not found elsewhere
  • Profit-Taker is the primary source of Toroids for Vox Solaris standing gains

Quick Reference

Overview
  • Location: Orb Vallis, Venus — accessible from Fortuna elevator
  • Profit-Taker access: Vox Solaris rank Old Mate (max rank) required for the heist mission series
  • Exploiter access: Complete Buried Debts quest; requires Diluted Thermia (from Thermia Fractures event or stockpile)
  • Recommended squad size: 2–4 players; Profit-Taker can be soloed with the right build; Exploiter is very soloable
  • Archgun required: Profit-Taker legs and pylons are immune to standard weapons; Archgun is mandatory

🔑 2. Access & Requirements

Profit-Taker Unlock Path

Access
  • Step 1: Reach Fortuna Standing rank Tide Turner (rank 4 of 5 with Solaris United)
  • Step 2: Reach Vox Solaris standing rank Old Mate (rank 5 of 5) — this requires large amounts of Toroids and gems from Orb Vallis
  • Step 3: Unlock Heist missions from Eudico in Fortuna — four heists, must be completed in order (Heist 1 → 4)
  • Heist 4 is the Profit-Taker fight itself; completing it once gives permanent access to replays via Bounties
  • Archgun Deployer must be equipped in your Gear wheel before entering the mission

Exploiter Orb Unlock Path

Access
  • Step 1: Complete the Buried Debts quest (available after Vox Solaris rank 1+)
  • Step 2: Obtain Diluted Thermia — from Thermia Fractures event (recurring), or stockpile from previous event runs
  • Step 3: Equip Diluted Thermia in Gear wheel before entering Orb Vallis
  • Location: Exploiter Orb roams near Enrichment Labs on the eastern side of Vallis; approach it to trigger the fight
  • No standing gate — only the quest completion and Thermia are required

🥇 3. Profit-Taker Phases

Phase 1 — Shield Cycling

Profit-Taker
  • Profit-Taker's body shield cycles through elements displayed on the HUD: Impact, Puncture, Slash, Heat, Cold, Electric, Toxin, Blast, Corrosive, Viral, Magnetic, Radiation, Gas
  • Match the displayed element with your weapon to strip the shield segment; wrong element does nothing
  • Meta approach: Run a loadout covering 4–5 elements across your weapons — swap quickly as the cycle changes
  • Archon mods and builds that swap elements on the fly (Stropha, Contagion, Fluctus) are popular choices
  • Phase ends when all shield HP is stripped; Profit-Taker becomes vulnerable briefly before re-shielding

Phase 2 — Archgun Legs

Profit-Taker
  • Profit-Taker's legs become the target; standard weapons cannot damage them — Archgun only
  • Deploy your Archgun via Gear wheel (Archwing Deployer) or Mech (Necramech); aim for the glowing leg joints
  • Best Archguns for legs: Velocitus (Magnetic — highest DPS vs Corpus), Larkspur Prime (Corrosive AoE), Mausolon (good all-around)
  • Pylons spawn around the arena — destroy them (Archgun required) or they prevent damage to the boss
  • Once all four legs are downed, a brief body-damage window opens before phase 3

Phase 3 & 4 — Repeat + Finale

Profit-Taker
  • Phases 3 and 4 repeat the shield-cycling and Archgun-leg pattern at increased intensity
  • Additional Corpus reinforcements and Nullifier bubbles increase pressure — have a CC subsume or frame ability to handle adds
  • Phase 4 body: After all leg phases, the Profit-Taker body becomes fully vulnerable; dump all remaining DPS into the torso
  • Time-to-kill record runs use Corrosive Projection auras + high burst Archgun to end Phase 4 in seconds
  • Death protection: Profit-Taker has a bleedout radius — if you go down near it, revive quickly or bleed out faster

General Tips

Profit-Taker
  • Corrosive Projection aura removes base armor from the Profit-Taker body — stack 4 in a squad for maximum effect
  • Knockdowns are constant — bring Handspring (Exilus) or use a frame with knockdown immunity (Rhino Iron Skin)
  • Rolling Guard is near-mandatory; the fight has many status procs and brief invulnerability windows help with repositioning
  • Archgun Ammo Mutation or Ammo Chain mod keeps Archgun fed during long phases
  • Operator Amp does nothing to the boss; focus operator on Vomvalysts or adds only

🔥 4. Profit-Taker Builds

Chroma — Solo Speed Build

Builds
  • Why Chroma: Vex Armor (3) grants massive weapon damage bonus on taking hits; Profit-Taker constantly deals damage so Vex stacks are always maxed
  • Key mods: High strength (Transient Fortitude + Blind Rage), Continuity, Primed Flow, Effigy augment for bonus damage
  • Weapon loadout: Primary = Corrosive element weapon, Secondary = Radiation/Viral, Melee = Contagion or high Toxin, Archgun = Velocitus or Larkspur
  • Aura: Corrosive Projection (removes Profit-Taker armor)
  • Fastest solo strategy: Use Vex Armor to boost Archgun damage to extreme levels during leg phases. Chroma currently holds many sub-3-minute solo records.

Rhino — Beginner-Friendly Build

Builds
  • Why Rhino: Iron Skin blocks all knockdown and status during the fight; Roar buffs both weapons and Archgun damage
  • Key mods: High strength for Iron Skin and Roar, Continuity, Ironclad Charge augment for huge Iron Skin pool
  • Weapon loadout: Cover 4+ elements across your loadout; prioritize Heat, Cold, Radiation, Viral, Toxin for common cycling elements
  • Aura: Corrosive Projection or Steel Charge
  • Playstyle: Keep Iron Skin active always — recast before it drops. Roar before opening Archgun for bonus damage. Much more forgiving than Chroma for learning the fight.

Archgun Recommendations

Builds
  • Velocitus (top pick): Magnetic damage — Corpus are weak to Magnetic; enormous single-target DPS on legs and body; requires charging
  • Mausolon: Auto-fire, consistent DPS, AoE on kill — great for clearing pylons and adds alongside boss damage
  • Larkspur Prime: Corrosive + AoE — hits multiple legs simultaneously; good for faster leg phases in squad
  • Key Archgun mods: Dual Rounds (multishot), Parallax Scope (crit chance), Automatic Trigger (fire rate), Ammo Chain (ammo efficiency)
  • All Archguns benefit from Necramech Mods if using Voidrig/Bonewidow during the fight

🔥 5. Exploiter Orb

Phase 1 — Coolant Vents

Exploiter
  • Exploiter Orb has a heat shield — standard weapons deal no damage until the shield is broken
  • Step 1: Pick up a Thermia canister from the ground near the fight area (they glow orange)
  • Step 2: Throw the canister at a Coolant Vent on the Exploiter's body (glowing blue panels) to crack it open and deal heat damage to the orb
  • Repeat with multiple canisters to push heat gauge to max; the Exploiter then ejects its heat shield in a burst — move away from the explosion
  • Diluted Thermia canisters found in your Gear are used to start the fight, not thrown — the throwable canisters spawn in the fight arena

Phase 2 — Exposed Core

Exploiter
  • After the heat purge, Exploiter Orb's cooling vents are exposed and vulnerable — deal damage to her back/underside to progress
  • She summons Coolant Raknoids (small enemies that restore her cooling) — kill them first or they reset progress
  • Damage her exposed vents with high-DPS weapons; this phase allows any damage type
  • After sufficient damage, she reroutes heat and Phase 1 begins again — repeat the thermia throw cycle
  • Final kill: On the last Phase 2, a large explosion ends the fight and drops all loot in a scatter pattern across the arena

Exploiter Tips & Best Frames

Exploiter
  • Best frames: Any tanky/sustain frame works well; Oberon, Rhino, Nidus handle the Raknoid swarms easily
  • Bring a fast primary for killing Coolant Raknoids — they die quickly but spawn frequently; Ignis Wraith or Kuva Bramma are popular
  • The fight takes place near environmental cryo-vents — stand in them if overheating becomes a threat (environmental heat proc causes damage over time)
  • Solo tip: Very doable solo; Phase 1 goes faster with more thermia throws, but the rhythm is easy to learn. Budget 6–10 minutes for solo runs.
  • Diluted Thermia source: Thermia Fractures events occur regularly and are the primary farming method; check the event calendar

🎁 6. Rewards

Profit-Taker Drops

Rewards
  • Toroids: Calda, Sola, and Vega Toroids — currency for Vox Solaris standing; also drop from enemies in specific Vallis zones but PT is the most reliable source
  • Archgun Rivens: Profit-Taker is one of the few sources of Archgun Riven Mods
  • Corpus mods: Various Corpus-specific mods drop from the loot table
  • Heist bounty bonus: Completing optional objectives in the heist (no alarms, time limit, etc.) gives bonus rewards including Vox Solaris standing and gems
  • Kitgun parts / Kitgun Riven Mods also appear in the reward pool

Exploiter Orb Drops

Rewards
  • Hildryn Blueprint & Components: All four Hildryn parts drop exclusively from Exploiter Orb (she cannot be obtained otherwise)
  • Gyromag, Atmo, Repeller Systems: Rare Fortuna crafting materials used for Vox Solaris standing and advanced Kitgun/Zaw crafting
  • Diluted Thermia: Small amounts drop from the fight itself (recoup the entry cost over multiple runs)
  • Corpus mods: Similar secondary drop table as other Vallis fights
  • Exploiter runs are very fast (5–8 min) and rewarding — the primary grind path for Hildryn and advanced Fortuna materials

Vox Solaris Standing Farming

Rewards
  • Vox Solaris standing is capped daily by your Mastery Rank; plan your Toroid turn-ins to not exceed the daily cap
  • Best Toroid farm: Profit-Taker heist rewards + Calda Toroid from Spaceport zone kills, Sola Toroid from Temple of Profit kills
  • To reach Old Mate quickly: farm Exploiter first (fast runs), use Gyromag Systems for large standing chunks, supplement with bounty completion
  • Profit-Taker Heist 4 rewards can be boosted with a Resource Booster — double Toroids per run
  • Nekros Desecrate does not work on boss bodies; brings no loot advantage on PT or Exploiter

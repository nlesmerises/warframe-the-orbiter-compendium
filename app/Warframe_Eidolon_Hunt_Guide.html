🌙 1. The Three Eidolons

Teralyst (Terry)

Entry
  • Entry-level Eidolon — 4 synovias (limbs) to destroy
  • Drops: Intact Sentient Cores, low-tier Arcanes
  • Capture (do not kill) to unlock Garuda
  • Required squad: Amp + Void Operator to break Void shield

Garuda (Garry)

Mid
  • Mid-tier — 6 synovias
  • Drops: Exceptional Sentient Cores, better Arcanes
  • Requires 2× charged Eidolon Lures from the Terry fight
  • Must capture Terry first in the same night cycle

Hydrolyst (Harry)

Hardest
  • Hardest — 6 synovias + AoE storm phases
  • Drops: Flawless/Perfect Sentient Cores, top Arcanes
  • Requires 3× charged lures from the Garuda fight
  • Storm phase: lightning strikes entire map — stay stacked on lures

📋 2. Pre-Hunt Requirements

Amp Setup

Gear
  • Mote Amp (starter) is too slow — upgrade at Quills in Cetus
  • Minimum viable: 223 Amp configuration
  • Recommended: 177 or x77 for consistent shield breaks
  • Only Operator Amp shots can break the Void shield — weapons cannot

Eidolon Lures

Gear
  • Blue glowing creatures found near water on the Plains
  • Charge each lure by killing 5 Vomvalysts near it
  • Terry: 2 charged lures to capture | 1 to kill
  • Garry: 2 lures | Harry: 3 lures — lures prevent teleport on capture

Operator / Focus School

Gear
  • Void Mode + Void Blast strips Eidolon Void shield
  • Madurai — Void Strike: charge in Void Mode then fire Amp for massive burst
  • Magus Lockdown (Operator Arcane): CC Vomvalysts during lure-charging
  • Zenurik or Unairu as secondary school options

Recommended Warframes

Gear
  • Chroma: Vex Armor + Effigy for team damage amplification
  • Trinity: Blessing for team heals and energy sustain
  • Volt: Electric Shield doubles Amp damage through the bubble
  • Harrow: Covenant gives team invincibility + crit bonus

⚔️ 3. Fight Sequence

Phase: Shield Break

Phase

Fire Void Strike Amp shot at the Eidolon after charging in Void Mode. Only Operator Amp shots work — weapons deal zero damage to the Void shield. Each shield phase must be broken before limbs can be damaged.

Phase: Synovia Destruction

Phase

Shoot the glowing synovias with your Warframe weapons. Destroy one at a time — the Eidolon regens its shield after each limb falls. Pick up the dropped shard before the next phase starts.

Phase: Final Collapse

Phase

After all synovias are destroyed the Eidolon collapses. Quickly shoot the glowing core on its chest to capture it. Capture always gives better rewards than killing. Charged lures must be within range or it teleports away.

Hydrolyst: Storm Phase

Phase

Between synovia phases Harry spawns a massive lightning storm across the Plains. Stand next to your charged lures — they absorb the lightning and protect players nearby. Spread out lures evenly around the squad.

🏆 4. Rewards

Sentient Cores

Rewards
  • Intact Core — Terry kill/capture → trade to Quills for standing
  • Exceptional Core — Garuda capture
  • Flawless Core — Hydrolyst capture
  • Perfect Core — Hydrolyst capture (rare) — highest Quills standing value

Arcane Drops

Rewards
  • Every Eidolon capture/kill drops Arcanes
  • Higher Eidolons drop rarer, more powerful Arcanes
  • Top drops: Arcane Energize, Arcane Grace, Arcane Guardian
  • Rank Arcanes: combine 5 copies of same rank to upgrade to next rank

Focus Points

Rewards
  • Best Focus-per-hour farm in the game during a night cycle
  • Equip Focus Lens on Warframe and weapons matching your school
  • Void Strike Amp shots grant bonus Focus on the kill
  • Experienced squad can run 5–6 Tridolons per night (50 min cycle)

🔬 6. Amp Naming System & Tier Guide

Amps are named by three digits: Prism – Scaffold – Brace (e.g. "177"). Each component is ranked 1–7 (Mote=0, Amps from Quills/Vox Solaris rank 1–7).

Starter Amp: Mote (000)

Amps
  • Default Amp from completing Saya’s Vigil quest
  • Very low damage — struggles to break Eidolon shields efficiently
  • Acceptable for Teralyst only in a pinch
  • Upgrade immediately at Quills in Cetus as your first priority

Recommended Amp: x77

Amps
  • Prism: Any (x = your choice) — Raplak (1) for sniping, Shwaak (2) for AoE
  • Scaffold 7: Klamora — sustained beam that melts Vomvalysts fast for lure charging
  • Brace 7: Certus — +20% Amp Crit Chance; best all-purpose brace
  • 177 or 277 covers all Eidolon content comfortably

Void Strike Technique

Amps
  • Enter Void Mode (Operator void form) and hold still — charges accumulate per second
  • Each second in Void Mode adds 5 stacks (cap: 12 stacks for ×6.25 multiplier)
  • Exit Void Mode and fire your Amp immediately — first shot consumes all stacks
  • With Madurai’s Void Strike passive, one shot can remove an Eidolon shield instantly

Night Cycle Timing

Amps
  • Plains night cycle lasts approximately 50 minutes real time
  • Day cycle is roughly 100 minutes — no Eidolons spawn during day
  • Cycle resets independent of login — check in-game Plains timer in Navigation
  • Experienced Tridolon squad can clear all 3 Eidolons in ~10–15 minutes
  • Beginners should target 1–2 Eidolons per night until mechanics are comfortable

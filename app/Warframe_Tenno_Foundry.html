⚗️ Elemental Combinations Reference

Viral (Cold + Toxin)

Multiplies damage dealt directly to health (up to +325% at 10 stacks). The current meta choice for almost all builds.

Corrosive (Electric + Toxin)

Strips target armor (up to 80% at 10 stacks), increasing all raw direct damage. Great against high-armor targets.

Radiation (Heat + Electric)

Forces enemies to attack each other and draw aggro away from players. Essential for bosses and Eidolons.

Magnetic (Cold + Electric)

Disrupts enemy shields, amplifying damage against them and preventing shield regeneration. Meta against Corpus.

Core Mod Combinations & Guidelines

Blood Rush + Organ Shatter

The critical scaling baseline for melee. Blood Rush pushes critical hit rates through the roof per combo multiplier, while Organ Shatter multiplies that critical damage.

Condition Overload + Weeping Wounds

The status synergy powerhouse. Weeping Wounds ensures constant status applications, triggering Condition Overload's massive base damage scaling per active status.

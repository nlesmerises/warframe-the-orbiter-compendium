🔮 1. Corrupted Mods (Deimos Vaults)

These mods offer a massive positive stat at the cost of a negative stat. They are the backbone of all endgame builds and easily sell for 15-20 Platinum each.

🛡️ Warframe Corrupted Mods

  • Blind Rage +Strength / -Efficiency
  • Transient Fortitude +Strength / -Duration
  • Narrow Minded +Duration / -Range
  • Fleeting Expertise +Efficiency / -Duration
  • Overextended +Range / -Strength

🔫 Weapon Corrupted Mods

  • Critical Delay +Crit Chance / -Fire Rate (Rifle)
  • Creeping Bullseye +Crit Chance / -Fire Rate (Pistol)
  • Vile Acceleration +Fire Rate / -Damage (Rifle)
  • Heavy Caliber +Damage / -Accuracy (Rifle)
  • Spoiled Strike +Damage / -Attack Speed (Melee)

⚖️ 2. Galvanized Mods (Arbitrations)

Purchased with Vitus Essence. Essential for Steel Path damage scaling. Duplicates sell for 20 Platinum each.

🔫 Rifles & Shotguns

  • Galvanized Chamber Rifle Multishot
  • Galvanized Aptitude Rifle Status DMG
  • Galvanized Scope Rifle Crit on Headshot
  • Galvanized Hell Shotgun Multishot
  • Galvanized Savvy Shotgun Status DMG

🔫 Pistols & Warframe

  • Galvanized Diffusion Pistol Multishot
  • Galvanized Shot Pistol Status DMG
  • Galvanized Crosshairs Pistol Crit on Headshot
  • Rolling Guard Warframe Invulnerability
  • Adaptation Warframe Damage Resistance

💱 Mod Trading & Ranks Quick Ref

Farming Tools: Warframe.market
Deimos Vaults: Requires Dragon Keys
Arbitrations: Unlocks after Star Chart
Ranks Efficiency Tip: Upgrading a 10-rank mod to Rank 8 costs only 25% of the total Endo and Credits needed to reach Rank 10, but provides 80% of the maximum strength. Farm duplicates to sell!

📊 7. Trading Value Tier Guide

⭐ S-Tier (100p+)

  • Primed Continuity, Primed Flow
  • Galvanized Aptitude, Galvanized Crosshairs
  • Blind Rage (corrupted)
  • Tempo Royale (stance)
  • Any rare Primed mod not recently in Baro rotation

🌟 A-Tier (30–100p)

  • Transient Fortitude, Overextended
  • All Galvanized weapon mods
  • Growing Power (aura)
  • Blind Justice, Crimson Dervish (stances)
  • Arcane Energize, Guardian (non-Baro source)

⭐ B-Tier (5–30p)

  • All 60/60 elemental mods (most weapons need them)
  • Common Corrupted mods (Fleeting Expertise, etc.)
  • Nightmare mods (Intensify variants)
  • Most Aura mods
  • Syndicate augments for popular frames

🔥 Damage Types & Status Effects

Core Concept
Status effects interact with enemy health layers — armor, shields, and base health, each with its own vulnerabilities.
The Meta Formula
Prime, strip, kill. Amplify damage to the health pool (Viral), remove the defensive layer in the way (Corrosive for armor, Cold/Magnetic for shields), then deliver a kill condition that bypasses what's left (Slash True Damage).
Core Pairing
Viral + Slash the standard endgame formula — prime with Viral, let Slash bleeds finish the kill.
Melee Note
Condition Overload multiplies melee damage per unique status type on the target — stack variety, not just one type.

🥽 Slash — The Kill Condition

True Damage — bypasses armor/shields35% base dmg/tick over 6sPairs with Viral

The undisputed king of damage at high enemy levels — armor scaling gets astronomical on Steel Path, so True Damage is often the only reliable kill path. Stack via Virulent Scourge or Primed Fever Strike on high status-chance weapons.

🦠 Viral — The Force Multiplier

Toxin + Cold3.25× health damage at 10 stacksAmplifies Slash bleed

The premier primer status. Prime the target with Viral stacks, then let Slash bleeds do the heavy lifting — the two are inseparable in meta builds.

⚗️ Corrosive — The Armor Stripper

Toxin + Electricity80% armor removal at 10 stacksPermanent, doesn't decay

Against heavily armored Grineer Lancers, Corrupted Heavy Gunners, and Steel Path Bombards, Corrosive stripping is the difference between tickling them and deleting them. Unlike Heat, stacks persist until the enemy dies.

🔥 Heat — The All-Rounder

50% of current armor/procDoT + panic (interrupts enemy)Best vs. Infested

Versatile and infinitely stackable. Shines against the Infested (no armor to worry about) and is a core piece of Condition Overload melee builds.

❄️ Cold — The Shield Shredder

+25% shield dmg/stack (5 max)Slows movement + attack rateHalf of Viral (Cold+Toxin)

The only status that directly weakens Corpus shields — the shield-layer equivalent of Corrosive. Essential against Sisters of Parvos and shielded Steel Path heavies.

☢️ Radiation — The Confusion Tool

Heat + ElectricityEnemies attack each otherMandatory vs. Acolytes/Sentients

Converts the battlefield into controlled chaos. In dense Steel Path tilesets, one proc on a heavy unit can neutralize an entire room while Slash/Viral do the actual killing under cover.

🧭 Magnetic — The Corpus Counter

Cold + Electricity-75% max shield capacity/stackPairs with Viral

Collapses the Corpus shield layer before your main damage connects. Stack on Nullifiers and Shield Ospreys, then Viral handles the residual health.

⚔️ Steel Meridian

"Protecting the weak"

🚀 Fastest Standing Method

  1. Pledge to Steel Meridian at the Syndicate console in your Orbiter
  2. Equip their highest-rank sigil to earn passive standing from all missions
  3. Run Syndicate Alert missions - collect all 8 medallions per run
  4. Turn in medallions at any Relay for bonus standing (bypasses daily cap!)
  5. Focus on Capture & Exterminate Syndicate missions for fastest clears

🏆 Key Rewards Worth Getting

  • Vaykor Hek - Powerful shotgun with syndicate proc (healing burst), one of the best syndicate weapons
  • Vaykor Marelok - Solid secondary pistol with Justice proc
  • Augment Mods - Scattered Justice (Hek), Iron Shrapnel (Rhino), Ironclad Charge (Rhino)
  • Large Team Energy Restore - Reusable blueprint, essential for hard content
  • Relic Packs - Good platinum sink for fissure farming

🌍 Open World Factions

Core Concept
Neutral factions, no alliance conflicts. Bounties Conservation Resources

📋 Recommended Priority Order

1ST
Entrati (Deimos)

Unlocks the Helminth system - the single biggest power boost in the game. Essential for endgame build diversity.

2ND
Holdfasts (Zariman)

Incarnon weapons and powerful Arcanes. Some of the strongest gear in the game is gated behind Holdfast ranks.

3RD
Cavia (Sanctum Anatomica)

Key for endgame melee builds. Melee Arcanes (like Melee Influence) are meta for maximizing damage.

4TH
Ostron & Solaris United

Important for early-to-mid-game: Zaws (custom melee), Kitguns (custom secondaries), and unlocking The Quills / Vox Solaris.

🌅

🎣 Ostron (Cetus)

Plains of Eidolon - Earth

🚀 Fastest Standing Methods

BEST
Bounties (Konzu)

Run highest-tier bounties you can handle. Each completion gives large standing chunks. Fast with a good squad. Steel Path bounties give even more.

GOOD
Animal Conservation (Ivara)

Use Tranq Rifle + Ivara's sleep arrow in Plains. Capture rare animals (Vasca Kavat, rare birds) for high standing per catch. Relaxed, solo-friendly, no combat required.

ALT
Fishing & Mining

Turn in fish to Fisher Hai-Luk or gems/ore to Old Man Suumbaat for standing. Doubles as resource gathering. Resource Booster doubles yields.

🏆 Key Rewards Worth Getting

  • Zaws - Custom-built melee weapons. Some of the strongest melee in the game with the right parts
  • Cetus Arcanes - Magus Elevate, Virtuos Strike, useful Operator arcanes
  • Mining/Fishing gear upgrades - Better cutters and spears unlock higher-tier resources
  • Companion Mods - Tek Assault, Tek Enhance for Kavat builds
  • Gara Blueprint - Available from bounty rewards

💡 Pro Tips

  • Higher bounty tiers give disproportionately more standing
  • Resource Booster doubles fish/mining yields
  • Night bounties give Eidolon-related bonus rewards
  • Reaching max Ostron rank unlocks access to The Quills content

🏭 Solaris United (Fortuna)

Orb Vallis - Venus

🚀 Fastest Standing Methods

BEST
Ticker's Debt-Bonds

Buy Debt-Bonds from Ticker (costs credits + resources) and turn them in for standing. Fastest method if you have spare credits. Stock rotates daily - check for cheap deals!

GOOD
Animal Conservation (Ivara)

Capture Kubrodon (highest value) using pheromones. Sleep with Ivara → Tranq. Perfect/Rare captures give 2-5x more standing. Very efficient per capture.

ALT
Bounties (Eudico)

Standard bounty farming from Eudico. Reliable standing and also drops Debt-Bonds + part rewards. Steel Path for extra gains.

🏆 Key Rewards Worth Getting

  • Kitguns - Custom-built secondaries/primaries. Catchmoon, Tombfinger, Gaze are all excellent. Some of the best secondaries in the game
  • MOA Companions - Build your own robot companion with unique precepts
  • Garuda Blueprint - Warframe available from bounty rewards
  • Companion Mods - Synth Reflex, Synth Fiber, Synth Deconstruct (meta companion mod set)
  • Mining/Fishing gear - Advanced Sunpoint Plasma Drill is essential for all open worlds

💡 Pro Tips

  • Ticker's stock rotates - check daily for cheap Debt-Bond deals
  • Rare/Perfect animal captures give 2-5x more standing
  • Orb Vallis bounties also drop Debt-Bonds as rewards
  • Ranking up unlocks access to Vox Solaris (Little Duck's back room)
  • The Sunpoint Plasma Drill has built-in radar - buy it early, use it everywhere

🧪 Entrati (Necralisk)

Cambion Drift - Deimos

🚀 Fastest Standing Methods

BEST
Mother Bounties → Token System

Run bounties from Mother to earn tokens. Trade tokens to Grandmother for standing. Tokens can be stockpiled indefinitely - effectively bypasses daily cap storage! Farm tokens on weekends, turn in daily.

GOOD
Family Tokens (Father/Daughter/Son/Otak)

Complete tasks for each family member to earn their specific tokens. Father = resources, Daughter = fish, Son = conservation, Otak = mining. Then trade at Grandmother for standing.

ALT
Conservation (Son)

Capture Deimos wildlife using Tranq Rifle. Trade perfect captures to Son for his tokens, then convert at Grandmother. Good for solo play.

🏆 Key Rewards Worth Getting

  • Helminth Segment - Rank 3 (Associate). THE most important reward - lets you swap abilities between Warframes. Absolute game-changer.
  • Xaku Blueprint - Unique Warframe with powerful abilities
  • Deimos Kitguns - Additional Kitgun parts with unique mechanics
  • Vulpaphyla/Predasite Companions - Infested pets, Panzer Vulpaphyla is one of the best companions in the game
  • Necramech Mods - Essential for Voidrig/Bonewidow optimization

💡 Pro Tips

  • The token system is unique - stockpile tokens, turn in at your pace
  • Grandmother converts ANY family token → Entrati standing
  • Required for Helminth Segment (Rank 3 Associate) - prioritize this!
  • Son sells Helminth Invigoration Segment at higher ranks for weekly buffs
  • Panzer Vulpaphyla is arguably the best companion - get it from Son

⚡ The Holdfasts (Zariman)

Zariman Ten Zero

🚀 Fastest Standing Methods

BEST
Zariman Bounties - Extermination (Quinn)

Run bounties from Quinn. Extermination is the fastest mission type for pure standing/hour. Void Flood is also fast. Steel Path gives boosted rewards.

GOOD
Void Angel Hunting

Defeating Void Angels during Zariman missions gives significant bonus standing on top of bounty rewards. Higher tiers drop Archon Shards too.

ALT
Void Cascade / Flood

These mission types are longer but offer more total standing per run. Good if you enjoy the gameplay loop and want resources alongside standing.

🏆 Key Rewards Worth Getting

  • Incarnon Weapon Adapters - Transform base weapons into Incarnon forms, some of the strongest weapons in the game
  • Gyre Blueprint - Powerful electric-themed Warframe from bounties
  • Zariman Arcanes - Powerful Warframe and Operator arcanes (Eternal, Emergence)
  • Evolved Amp Parts - Superior Amp components compared to Quills/Vox Solaris
  • Focus Lens Blueprints - For Operator progression
  • Archon Shards - Dropped by Void Angels, permanent Warframe stat boosts

💡 Pro Tips

  • Steel Path Zariman bounties give significantly increased rewards
  • Void Angels drop Archon Shards at higher tiers - always kill them
  • Extermination is fastest for pure standing/hour
  • Zariman Amp parts are better than Quills/Vox Solaris amps - skip old amp grind
  • Incarnon weapons are current endgame meta - prioritize adapters

Deimos - Requires "Whispers in the Walls" quest

🚀 Fastest Standing Methods

BEST
Cavia Bounties (Highest Tier)

Complete bounties in the Sanctum Anatomica. Higher tiers give significantly more standing. Fast mission types like Extermination are ideal.

GOOD
Alchemy Missions

Run Alchemy mission type for both standing and rare resource drops. Longer per run but more total standing + bonus loot.

ALT
Deep Archimedea (Weekly)

Complete the weekly Deep Archimedea challenge for a large standing bonus that resets each week. Also drops rare rewards.

🏆 Key Rewards Worth Getting

  • Melee Arcanes - Melee Influence and others are current meta for maximizing melee weapon damage
  • Qorvex Blueprint - Unique Warframe with powerful radiation abilities, available from bounties
  • New Arcanes - Several powerful arcanes unique to this syndicate
  • Onos Blueprints - Endgame weapon components
  • Cavia Cosmetics - Unique syandanas and armor sets

💡 Pro Tips

  • Must complete "Whispers in the Walls" quest to access this faction
  • Deep Archimedea weekly resets give large bonus standing
  • Melee Arcanes from Cavia are best-in-slot for melee builds - high priority
  • Has a "pity" system - buy specific Warframe/weapon parts directly with standing instead of relying on RNG drops
  • Steel Path versions give boosted rewards

Core Concept
Unique factions with specialized standing methods. All neutral — no alliance conflicts.

📋 Recommended Priority Order

1ST
Cephalon Simaris

2ND
Necraloid

📡 Cephalon Simaris

Any Relay - Sanctuary

🚀 Fastest Standing Methods

BEST
Stealth Scanning - Hits cap in ~5 minutes!

Use Ivara or Loki (invisible) in Plains of Eidolon on Steel Path. Fly high in Archwing, enable "Enemy Highlights" in display settings, scan enemies from above. Stealth scans give massive bonus standing. The fastest standing farm in the entire game.

GOOD
Daily Synthesis Target

Always accept the daily target from Simaris before doing anything else. Bonus standing does NOT count toward daily cap - it's free extra standing every day.

ALT
Sanctuary Onslaught

Running SO/ESO gives passive Simaris standing while also farming focus, relics, and Warframe parts (Khora from SO, Braton/Lato Vandal from ESO).

🏆 Key Rewards Worth Getting (Buy These First!)

  • Sol-Battery Widget - Unlimited Synthesis Scanner charges forever. Buy this FIRST.
  • Cross-Matrix Widget - Chance to double scans. Essential QoL.
  • Vector-Thread Widget - Faster scanning speed.
  • Simulacrum Key - Unlocks the Simulacrum, essential for build testing and DPS checks.
  • Health Conversion - Meta mod for armor-stacking frames (Nekros, Inaros).
  • Energy Conversion - +50% power strength on next ability cast. Great for nuke builds.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Get ALL three Scanner Widgets before anything else - they're permanent upgrades
  • Stealth scans give 2x standing compared to alerted scans
  • Health Conversion and Energy Conversion sell well on trade for platinum
  • Simulacrum lets you spawn any scanned enemy to test builds against

🌙 The Quills

Cetus - Operator Room (enter in Operator mode)

🚀 Fastest Standing Methods

BEST
Eidolon Hunts (Tridolon)

Hunt Teralyst → Gantulyst → Hydrolyst at night in the Plains. Trade Sentient Cores to Onkko: Brilliant Shards = 25,000, Radiant Shards = massive standing. One full Tridolon can nearly cap you.

GOOD
Teralyst Only (Solo-Friendly)

If you can't do full Tridolons, just farm the basic Teralyst. Easier, soloable, still gives decent cores. Multiple runs per night cycle.

ALT
Vomvalyst Farming

Kill Vomvalysts at night for Intact Sentient Cores (500 standing each). Much easier but much slower. Good for absolute beginners.

🏆 Key Rewards Worth Getting

  • Amp Parts (Prisms/Scaffolds/Braces) - Build better Amps for Operator combat. Note: Zariman amps are now superior, so don't over-invest here.
  • Virtuos Arcanes - Amp arcanes that boost Operator damage. Niche but useful for Eidolons.
  • Magus Arcanes - Operator arcanes like Magus Elevate (instant health on transference). Very useful.
  • Cetus Wisps - Can be purchased here, needed for many crafting recipes.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Enter The Quills room by switching to Operator mode near the Cetus door
  • Narmer Isoplasts (from Narmer bounties) can also be traded for Quills standing
  • Zariman Amps are now better - don't grind Quills extensively just for amps
  • Eidolon Hunts are also a great source of Focus and Arcane Energize
  • Recommended frames for Eidolons: Volt, Trinity, Harrow, Chroma/Rhino

🌏 Vox Solaris

Fortuna - Little Duck's Back Room

🚀 Fastest Standing Methods

BEST
Exploiter Orb Boss Fight

Run the Exploiter Orb boss (start from Deck 12 in Fortuna). Drops Lazulite Toroids worth massive standing + rare resources. One of the best standing/hour in the game. Repeatable.

GOOD
Toroid Farming (AFK-able)

Farm Toroids at specific locations: Spaceport (Vega), Enrichment Labs (Calda), Temple of Profit (Sola). Use Nekros + Hydroid/Khora for boosted drops. Trade Toroids to Little Duck.

ALT
Profit-Taker Orb Boss

Faster than Exploiter but harder and requires max Solaris United rank. Drops Toroids and credits. Speed-runners can clear in under 5 minutes.

🏆 Key Rewards Worth Getting

  • Amp Parts - Alternative Amp components. Note: Zariman amps are now better for most builds.
  • Baruuk Blueprint - Powerful pacifist Warframe, requires Vox Solaris rank
  • Operator Arcanes - Magus Lockdown, Magus Repair (niche but useful)
  • Hildryn Blueprint - Shield-based Warframe, very tanky

💡 Pro Tips

  • Narmer Isoplasts (from Narmer bounties) can also be traded for Vox Solaris standing
  • Exploiter Orb also drops Hildryn parts - double value per run
  • Nekros + Smeeta Kavat + Resource Booster = maximum Toroid drops
  • Most Vox Solaris Arcanes are outclassed now - only grind this if you need specific items
  • Requires Solaris United Old Mate rank to access Little Duck's back room

🪖 Kahl's Garrison

Weekly Break Narmer Missions

🚀 Fastest Standing Methods

BEST
Weekly Break Narmer Mission + ALL Side Objectives

Complete Kahl's weekly mission with every optional objective completed. Side objectives give massive bonus standing. Use a guide for objective locations the first time. Resets every week.

GOOD
Repeat Missions for Missed Objectives

If you missed side objectives, you can replay the mission to complete them. Standing is awarded per objective, not per completion.

🏆 Key Rewards Worth Getting

  • Archon Shards - MAIN REASON to do this. Permanent stat boosts for your Warframe. Extremely powerful endgame progression.
  • Styanax Blueprint - Warframe available from Kahl's shop (Chipper)
  • Archon Mods - Powerful unique mods (Archon Stretch, Archon Vitality, etc.)
  • Kahl Cosmetics - Unique armor and syandana sets
  • Weapon Blueprints - Several unique weapons from Chipper's shop

💡 Pro Tips

  • Side objectives give significantly more standing than just completing the mission
  • Stock (currency) earned is spent at Chipper's shop in the Drifter's Camp
  • Weekly cap - no need to grind daily, just complete once per week
  • Archon Shards are the most impactful endgame power increase per time invested
  • Use YouTube guides for side objective locations until you memorize them
  • Requires completion of "Veilbreaker" quest to access

🛫 Ventkids

Fortuna - Vent Hideout

🚀 Fastest Standing Methods

BEST
K-Drive Tricks - The Pearl Grinding Spot

Ride your K-Drive in the Orb Vallis. Go to The Pearl (the large pipe near Fortuna entrance) and chain grinds + flips for massive trick combos. Score multipliers stack - one good combo can give huge standing.

GOOD
K-Drive Races

Complete K-Drive race courses scattered around Orb Vallis. Decent standing per race, and they're short. Good for variety.

ALT
Free Roam Tricks

Just ride around Orb Vallis doing tricks on any surface. Slower but zero-effort. Listen to music and vibe.

🏆 Key Rewards Worth Getting

  • K-Drive Parts - Mostly mastery fodder (MR points). Build all 4 boards for mastery.
  • K-Drive Mods - Maglocks and Inertia Dampeners are the only widely useful ones
  • K-Drive Skins - Cosmetic customization for your board
  • Mastery Rank XP - Each K-Drive gives 6,000 MR. Worth building for MR progression.

💡 Pro Tips

📻 Nightwave

Nora Night - Orbiter Radio

🚀 Fastest Standing Methods

BEST
Elite Weekly Acts (7,000 standing each)

Complete all Elite Weekly challenges first - they give the most standing per act. Examples: Complete a Sortie, Kill a Profit-Taker, complete 3 Nightmare missions.

GOOD
Regular Weekly Acts (4,500 standing each)

Complete all regular weekly acts next. Usually easier tasks like: Kill 150 enemies with a specific element, complete X bounties, gild a modular item.

ALT
Daily Acts (1,000 standing each)

Small daily tasks. Quick to complete but lowest standing. Do them if you're already online, don't stress about missing them.

🏆 Key Rewards Worth Getting

  • Nightwave Creds - Spend on: Nitain Extract (essential crafting material), Orokin Catalysts/Reactors ("Potatoes"), Aura Mods, Alternate Helmets
  • Nitain Extract - Almost impossible to get elsewhere. Buy with Nightwave Creds. Always stock up.
  • Orokin Catalysts/Reactors - "Blue/Gold Potatoes". Double mod capacity on weapons/Warframes.
  • Umbra Forma - Appears at high ranks. Lets you put Umbral polarity on any slot. Extremely rare and valuable.
  • Exclusive Cosmetics - Armor sets, syandanas, weapon skins from each season's track.
  • Kuva - Buy with excess Nightwave Creds for Riven mod rolling.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Weekly acts give 4,500 standing each, Elite Weeklies give 7,000 each
  • Missed weeks CAN be recovered - old acts roll into the current week's pool
  • Nitain Extract should be your #1 Cred purchase - you'll always need more
  • After Rank 30 (max), you earn bonus Nightwave Creds per rank - infinite value
  • No daily cap - Nightwave standing is purely act-based, complete at your pace
  • Nightwave seasons run for months - you don't need to rush

⚡ Universal Standing Tips

Core Concept
Daily cap formula, medallion tricks, token systems, and efficiency strategies.
📊 Daily Cap Formula
Your daily cap = 1,000 + (MR × 1,000). Higher Mastery Rank = more standing per day.
🎍 Medallions Bypass Cap
Syndicate medallions do NOT count toward your daily limit. Farm them for unlimited bonus standing.
🪙 Token Systems
Entrati tokens can be stockpiled and turned in later, letting you bank standing beyond the daily cap.
👑 Steel Path Bonus
Many activities give more standing or better rewards when done on Steel Path difficulty.
💱 Trading Saves Time
All syndicate rewards are tradeable. If you need a mod from an enemy faction, just trade with another player for platinum.
📌 Pick 4 Core Syndicates
You can maintain 4 of 6 core syndicates positive simultaneously. Best combos: Steel Meridian + Red Veil Suda + New Loka
📡 Loot Radar Stacking
Stack Loot Detector Thief's Wit Animal Instinct for maximum radar range when hunting medallions.
💡 Don't Burn Out
Focus on 1-2 factions at a time. Hit daily cap → move on. Consistency beats marathon grinding sessions.

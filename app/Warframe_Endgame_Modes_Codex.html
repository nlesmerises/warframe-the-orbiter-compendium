⚠️ 1. Arbitrations (The Pre-Endgame)

Unlocked by clearing every node on the normal Star Chart. Arbitrations are hourly alerts featuring standard mission types but with severe lethal modifiers.

📜 The Rules of Arbitration

  • No Self-Revives: If you die, you are dead. You leave behind a tower. Teammates must collect "Resurgence Tokens" dropped by Arbitration Drones to revive you.
  • Arbitration Shield Drones: These red drones attach to enemies and make them 100% immune to abilities and damage. You must shoot the drone first to kill the enemies underneath.
  • Massive Buffs: Every hour, one Warframe and two weapons get +300% Strength and +500% Health.

💰 The Loot (Vitus Essence)

Every rotation drops Vitus Essence. Take this to the Arbiters of Hexis room in any Relay to buy the most important mods in the game:

  • Galvanized Mods: Massive stacking damage buffs for guns.
  • Adaptation: Builds up to 90% damage resistance.
  • Rolling Guard: Dodge rolling grants 3 seconds of invincibility.

⚔️ 2. The Steel Path (True Endgame)

After clearing the Star Chart, talk to Teshin in any Relay to unlock The Steel Path. This adds a toggle to your Star Chart that lets you replay the entire game on "Hard Mode."

⚔️ The Steel Path Modifiers

  • Level +100: Every enemy starts 100 levels higher than normal.
  • +250% Defenses: Enemies have +250% Health, Armor, and Shields. You MUST use armor stripping (Corrosive/Heat) or armor bypassing (Slash/Viral) to kill them.
  • Resource Boost: Permanent +100% Resource Drop Chance and Mod Drop Chance for the entire squad.

Acolytes & Steel Essence

  • Every 3-7 minutes in a Steel Path mission, a mini-boss called an Acolyte spawns.
  • Killing them drops Steel Essence and a Primary/Secondary Weapon Arcane (e.g., Primary Merciless).
  • Take Steel Essence to Teshin to buy Umbra Forma, Riven Mods, and Kuva.

✅ 3. Endgame Preparation Checklist

⚠️ Before You Dive In

Checklist

Arbitrations and Steel Path are unforgiving. Double-check this list before queuing up:

  • Armor Strip: Do you have a way to fully strip Grineer armor? (e.g., Unairu, Pillage, Terrify, or Corrosive/Heat procs).
  • Survivability: Can you survive a sudden 15,000 damage spike? (e.g., Shield Gating, Mesmer Skin, 90% Damage Reduction abilities).
  • Primer: Do you have a secondary weapon (like the Kuva Nukor) to spray enemies with statuses before hitting them with Condition Overload?
  • Companion: Is your Panzer Vulpaphyla equipped, or do you have a reliable way to keep your Sentinel alive?
  • Operator: Are your Magus Elevate or Magus Repair arcanes maxed out for instant emergency healing?

What this is

An offline reference and progress companion for Warframe: guides, databases, planners and trackers, all linked from the main hub.

Getting started

  • Open !Warframe_Master_Index.html to reach the main hub. Everything links from there.
  • No internet connection and no installation are required; it runs entirely in your browser.

Navigation

  • 🏠 Home (top left) returns to the hub.
  • ⚙️ Settings opens backup, restore and display options.
  • 🗺️ Site Map (top left, just past Settings) opens a grouped map of every page.
  • The quick-nav emoji row jumps straight to each main area: Getting Started, Arsenal, Mods & Arcanes, Farming & Economy, Combat & Mechanics, Endgame and Tools.
  • Prev / Next Chapter (top right, on the guide pages) move through the guides in sequence.

Your data

All progress (loadouts, checkboxes, custom entries) is saved locally in your browser and stays on your machine. Use Settings > Export Backup to save a copy, and Load Backup to restore it on this or another device.

Documentation & updates

  • 📋 Release Notes — full version history and changelog.
  • 📜 License Agreement — terms of use for this compendium.
  • ⚙️ Settings — manage your profile, backup your data, and adjust display preferences.

Sharing & distribution

The compendium is distributed as a self-contained zip. Unzip it anywhere and open !Warframe_Master_Index.html in any modern browser. No server required. Share the zip with clanmates so they can run their own local copy — all data stays on their own device.

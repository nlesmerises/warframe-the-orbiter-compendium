🪖 1. What Is Veilbreaker?

Play as Kahl-175

Overview
  • Veilbreaker is the quest and ongoing system where you play as Kahl-175, a freed Grineer soldier — not a Warframe.
  • Kahl uses guns, grenades, and stolen abilities in a weekly solo mission called Break Narmer.
  • It's a low-pressure weekly that feeds straight into endgame power via Archon Shards.

Why Do It Weekly

Overview
  • Stock — weekly currency spent at the Garrison for rewards.
  • Reliable path toward Archon Hunt resources and account-wide upgrades.
  • Fun change of pace — a stealthy, gun-focused soldier loadout.

🔓 2. How to Unlock It

📋 Prerequisites

  • Complete The New War quest — this introduces Kahl and unlocks Veilbreaker.
  • Play the short Veilbreaker quest from the Codex / inbox to open the Kahl's Garrison hub.
  • After that, Break Narmer becomes a repeatable weekly mission.

🪖 The Garrison Hub

Kahl's Garrison is the home base where you start the weekly mission, spend Stock at Chipper, and track progress. Access it from Navigation once unlocked.

🔄 3. The Weekly Loop

  • Each week a new Break Narmer mission rotates in — a solo objective run as Kahl.
  • Complete objectives and gather collectibles to earn Stock (the cap resets weekly).
  • Spend Stock at Chipper in the Garrison for the rotating reward catalog.
  • Veilbreaker also ties into the weekly Archon Hunt for Archon Shards.

🗡 5. Kahl's Kit

Weapons & Grenades

Kit
  • Kahl runs Grineer weapons and a grenade slot rather than Warframe powers.
  • Straightforward gunplay — aim, suppress, and clear objectives.

Stolen Abilities

Kit
  • Kahl can seize enemy abilities (e.g. turret control, sensor pulses) during the mission.
  • Use them to solve the room puzzles and reach hidden Stockpiles.

⛧ 6. Rewards & Archon Shards

  • Chipper's catalog — spend Stock on resources, arcanes, cosmetics, and useful materials.
  • Archon Shards — the headline reward from the weekly Archon Hunt; slot them into Warframes for permanent stat boosts.
  • Plan shard colors against your build — see the Archon Planner.
  • Great steady source of otherwise grindy materials with almost no risk.

💡 7. Tips

  • Do it once a week — Stock and Archon Hunt both reset weekly; don't leave them on the table.
  • Explore for every Stockpile before extracting — that's the bulk of your Stock.
  • Pair the weekly with your Archon Hunt and reset chores — see the Weekly Reset Checklist.
  • Spend Stock before it caps; unspent earnings above the weekly limit are wasted.

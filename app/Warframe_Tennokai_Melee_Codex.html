⚔️ Tennokai & Melee Arcanes

Playstyle recommendations with the unlock, farming, adapter, and installation path.

🔓 How to Unlock Tennokai and Melee Arcanes

Unlock the System: Complete Whispers in the Walls and gain access to the Sanctum Anatomica and Cavia systems.
Tennokai Mods: The quest introduces Tennokai. Farm additional Tennokai mods from Alchemy mission rotations in the Sanctum Anatomica, or trade with another player.
Melee Exilus Slot: Install a Melee Exilus Adapter on the weapon, then place the Tennokai mod in the unlocked Exilus slot.
Melee Arcanes: Farm Sanctum Anatomica missions and enemies, Netracells, or higher Cavia activities. Trading and Arcane Dissolution can fill missing ranks when available.
Melee Arcane Slot: Install a Melee Arcane Adapter on the weapon, then equip the chosen Arcane and combine duplicate copies to increase its rank.
Use in Combat: Build combo with normal attacks. When the Tennokai prompt appears, use Heavy Attack during the window for a fast heavy strike without consuming combo.

📖 What is Tennokai?

Tennokai is a system introduced in Whispers in the Walls. By equipping a Tennokai mod in your melee Exilus slot, you gain a chance to trigger a "Tennokai Opportunity" while attacking. If you perform a Heavy Attack during this window, it executes instantly, deals massive damage, and does not consume your Combo Multiplier!

Saryn Prime Vaulted

Lore / Role: Saryn is a lethal spreader of disease, acting as the undisputed queen of area-of-effect damage. By infecting a single target with her Spores and popping them, she dynamically spreads a devastating plague across the entire battlefield.

Passive Ability: Status effects inflicted upon enemies last 25% longer.

Stats (Rank 30): 300 / 300 / 300 / 225 / 1.00 (HP/Shield/Armor/Energy/Speed)

How to Acquire: Currently Vaulted. Must be acquired from vaulted Void Relics, player trading, or Prime Resurgence.

Procedure: Prime: 1x Blueprint, 1x Neuroptics, 1x Chassis, 1x Systems. Standard: 1x Blueprint (Market), plus components.

Advanced Arsenal Data

Active Abilities: Spores (Corrosive spread), Molt (Speed/Decoy), Toxic Lash (Toxin buff), Miasma (Viral Nuke).

Helminth Synergy: Roar (replaces Molt) for extreme Spore damage scaling, or Nourish for energy economy.

Signature Weapons: Spira Prime, Nikana Prime.

Recommended Arcanes: Molt Augmented (Strength), Arcane Energize (Energy).

📋 1. Overview

Prerequisites

Overview
  • Complete the Whispers in the Walls quest
  • Reach sufficient Cavia Standing rank to unlock missions
  • Access via Loid in the Necralisk (Deimos) lower level
  • Recommended: Archon Hunt and Steel Path experience before attempting

Weekly Reset

Overview
  • Resets every Sunday at midnight UTC
  • 3 missions per week — must complete in order (1 → 2 → 3)
  • Each mission has unique restrictions and modifiers that rotate
  • Restrictions apply to your entire loadout — plan all 3 before building

Reward Tiers

Overview
  • 1 mission: Entry reward (resources, standing)
  • 2 missions: Mid-tier reward (mods, Arcane fragments)
  • All 3: Full reward + guaranteed Archon Shard
  • Exclusive Arcanes only obtainable from Deep Archimedea

🚫 2. Common Restrictions & Counters

No Abilities / Abilities Restricted

Restriction

Warframe abilities are disabled entirely. Focus on weapon damage. Use Arcane Energize to maintain other buffs. Lavos and Hildryn are exceptions — Lavos uses cooldowns (not energy) and Hildryn uses Shields.

Specific Weapon Type Only

Restriction

Only weapons of one category (Rifles, Shotguns, Bows, Melee, etc.) deal damage. Build your strongest weapon of that type. Melee-only runs favor Blood Rush/Weeping Wounds combo builds.

Companion Restricted

Restriction

No Sentinel, Pet, or MOA. Build your Warframe to be fully self-sufficient. Arcane Energize provides energy without Carrier. Use a frame with built-in armor or health sustain.

Reduced Energy / No Energy Regen

Restriction

Energy orbs give little or nothing. Use Gear wheel energy restores (Ciphers, Team Energy Restores). Zenurik Wellspring helps. Hunter Adrenaline / Rage converts damage taken to energy — pairs with tanky frames.

Knockdown Amplified

Restriction

Enemies knock you down frequently. Equip Fortitude mod, Primed Sure Footed, or bring Rhino/Inaros for innate knockdown resistance. Knockdown immune Arcanes also help.

Necramech Required

Restriction

Portions of the mission require piloting a Necramech. Bring a leveled Bonewidow or Voidrig with health and armor mods. Arquebex (Voidrig's heavy weapon) is strong for boss phases.

🎯 3. Recommended Loadout Picks

No-Ability Frames

Loadout
  • Inaros: Massive health pool, self-revive from sand, no energy needed
  • Nidus: Stack-based survivability, mutation passive keeps him alive
  • Revenant: Mesmer Skin blocks damage without abilities if pre-cast
  • Lavos: Abilities run on cooldowns — completely immune to energy restrictions

Strong Weapon Picks

Loadout
  • Rifle: Cedo, Felarx, Incarnon Braton/Boltor
  • Secondary: Kuva Nukor, Tenet Cycron, Incarnon Lex
  • Melee: Heavy attack builds with Incarnon adapters (Hate, Dokrahm)
  • Bow: Kuva Bramma for AoE, Nataruk for precision damage

Exclusive Rewards

Loadout
  • Arcane Reaper — unique to Deep Archimedea
  • Grimoire Incarnon upgrades (Paragraphs) — only source
  • Archon Shard (guaranteed on full clear)
  • Cavia Standing and Voca (Whispers in the Walls resource)

💎 5. Archon Shard Reference

Archon Shards are socketed into Warframes at Helminth to grant permanent passive bonuses. Each Warframe can hold up to 5 shards. Shards come in 5 colors, each tied to an Archon boss.

🔴 Crimson Shard — Archon Amar

Shard
  • Ability Strength — flat bonus to power strength %
  • Casting Speed — faster ability animations
  • Melee Crit Chance — direct melee crit % increase
  • Tauforged (orange): 50% stronger effect per shard slot

🔵 Amber Shard — Archon Boreal

Shard
  • Ability Efficiency — reduces energy cost
  • Initial Energy — start missions with bonus energy
  • Energy Max — larger energy pool
  • Tauforged: Best for ability-heavy frames needing energy sustain

🔸 Azure Shard — Archon Nira

Shard
  • Health — flat health % increase
  • Shield Capacity — larger shield pool
  • Shield Recharge Rate — faster shield recovery
  • Tauforged: Best for shield-gating builds (Hildryn, Harrow)

🟢 Topaz Shard — Archon Amar (alt)

Shard
  • Toxin Damage — bonus Toxin to all attacks
  • Void Damage — bonus Void damage (Operator synergy)
  • Status Duration — longer status effect durations
  • Tauforged: Niche but strong on specific elemental builds

🟣 Violet Shard — Archon Nira (alt)

Shard
  • Ability Range — larger AoE on abilities
  • Ability Duration — longer lasting buffs/debuffs
  • Knockdown Resistance — direct immunity option
  • Tauforged: Best for support/CC frames (Khora, Nova)

🔥 Tauforged Shards

Shard
  • Rare variant of each shard color — 50% more powerful effect
  • Obtained only from completing all 3 Deep Archimedea missions in a week
  • Cannot be traded — must be earned personally
  • Best socket strategy: 1–2 Tauforged in key slots, rest normal shards

🎯 6. Mission Types & Cavia Syndicates

Mission Objectives

Missions
  • Exterminate: Kill all enemies — straightforward but restricted loadout complicates it
  • Survival: Survive for a set duration with life support drain
  • Defense: Protect a target through multiple waves
  • Excavation: Protect excavation drills through multiple digs
  • Mission type is revealed before entry alongside the restriction

Cavia Standing

Missions
  • Cavia is the Deimos research group — reached via Whispers in the Walls quest
  • Earn standing via Deep Archimedea completion and weekly Deimos missions
  • Voca items (drops from Murmur enemies) convert to Cavia standing
  • Higher Cavia rank unlocks better access tiers for Deep Archimedea rewards

Pre-Run Checklist

Missions
  • ✅ Check all 3 restrictions before choosing loadout
  • ✅ Bring Team Energy Restores if energy-restricted
  • ✅ Confirm your Necramech is modded if Mech segment appears
  • ✅ Pack Primed Sure Footed if knockdown vulnerability is active
  • ✅ Run solo or with trusted squad — random squads may abandon mid-run

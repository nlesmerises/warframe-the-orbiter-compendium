📋 1. Overview

What Are These Weapons?

Overview
  • Kuva weapons — obtained by vanquishing a Kuva Lich; Grineer faction; unique high-stat variants of existing weapons
  • Tenet weapons — obtained by vanquishing a Sister of Parvos; Corpus faction; unique variants with strong base stats
  • Both weapon types have an elemental bonus (25–60%) that adds a flat damage percentage of a chosen element
  • Both can be traded between players (with or without a Lich/Sister attached)
  • Both have a max rank of 40 (5 additional Forma beyond rank 30); each rank-5 interval grants a bonus level and stat increase

Why Farm Them?

Overview
  • Kuva and Tenet weapons have higher base damage than their standard counterparts — in many cases by 50–100%
  • The elemental bonus is permanent and stacks with mods, effectively giving a free 25–60% elemental damage mod slot
  • Several are outright best-in-class weapons at MR 5+ (Kuva Bramma, Kuva Zarr, Tenet Arca Plasmor, etc.)
  • Mastery Rank fodder — each unique weapon + element combo gives separate MR XP when leveled

💥 2. Kuva Weapon Farming

Step 1 — Spawn a Larvling

Kuva Lich
  • Go to any level 20+ Grineer mission (not Assassination, not Archwing)
  • Kill enemies until a Kuva Larvling spawns — it will appear with a glowing tether; do not kill it yet
  • Walk up to the Larvling to see a weapon preview floating above it — this shows which Kuva weapon it will become
  • If you want a different weapon, leave the mission (abort, not extract) and try again; the weapon type is randomized each spawn
  • Once you see the weapon you want, mercy kill the Larvling to create your Kuva Lich

Step 2 — Hunt the Lich

Kuva Lich
  • Your Lich will now occupy a controlled planet — missions on that planet show a Lich symbol
  • Run missions on the controlled planet to earn Murmur progress (kills reveal Requiem hints)
  • Each Lich requires 3 correct Requiem mods in the right sequence to be vanquished or converted
  • See the Lich & Sisters Guide for full Requiem and Murmur farming details
  • When you have all 3 Requiem mods identified, attempt a final confrontation on the Lich's planet

Step 3 — Vanquish or Convert

Kuva Lich
  • Vanquish — kills the Lich and gives you its weapon immediately
  • Convert — turns the Lich into an ally you can summon in missions (no weapon reward unless traded)
  • Vanquish for the weapon; Convert only if you specifically want that Lich as an ally or for trading
  • After vanquishing, the weapon appears in your Foundry — built and ready, no blueprint needed
  • The elemental bonus (25–60%) and element type are fixed at Larvling spawn — you cannot change them afterward (only Valence Fusion can increase the %)

Kuva Weapon List

Kuva Lich
  • 🔥 Kuva Bramma — explosive bow; massive AoE; top AoE primary
  • 🔥 Kuva Zarr — cannon/shotgun hybrid; devastating AoE
  • 🔥 Kuva Nukor — beam secondary; chains to 5 enemies; best status secondary
  • 🔥 Kuva Grattler — Archwing cannon; available in normal missions too
  • 🔥 Kuva Chakkhurr — single-shot rifle; innate radiation; Eidolon utility
  • 🔥 Kuva Kohm — shotgun with escalating fire rate; shreds with status
  • 🔥 Kuva Quartakk / Kuva Karak / Kuva Hind / Kuva Tonkor — solid mid-tier picks
  • 🔥 Kuva Ogris / Kuva Drakgoon / Kuva Ayanga — niche/fun options

💫 3. Tenet Weapon Farming

Step 1 — Spawn a Candidate

Tenet
  • Go to any level 20+ Corpus mission (not Railjack, not Archwing)
  • Kill enemies until a Candidate spawns — a named Corpus Crewman with a tether
  • Walk up to see the weapon preview — same mechanic as Kuva Larvlings
  • Abort and retry if you want a different weapon type; mercy kill when you see the one you want
  • Your Sister of Parvos is now created

Step 2 — Hunt the Sister

Tenet
  • Same structure as Lich hunting — controlled planet, Murmur farming, 3 Requiem mods
  • Sisters use Hound companions that must be defeated before the Sister can be confronted
  • The Hound drops a unique mod when defeated — worth keeping; they are tradeable
  • Sisters have different Requiem mods than Liches — Oull is the wildcard mod that works in any slot position
  • See the Lich & Sisters Guide for full progression details

Step 3 — Vanquish or Convert

Tenet
  • Same choice as Lich — Vanquish for the weapon, Convert for an ally
  • Converted Sisters can be traded; the receiver gets the Sister plus whatever Tenet weapon it holds
  • Tenet weapons appear in the Foundry after vanquishing — no blueprint needed
  • Unlike Kuva weapons, some Tenet weapons are also sold by Ergo Glast in Relay Void Trader rotations — check his stock before farming if you need one urgently

Tenet Weapon List

Tenet
  • 💫 Tenet Arca Plasmor — shotgun; wide beam; excellent status and neutral damage
  • 💫 Tenet Flux Rifle — rapid beam rifle; shreds armor with continuous fire
  • 💫 Tenet Diplos — auto-targeting secondary pistols; homing projectiles
  • 💫 Tenet Cycron — beam secondary; infinite punch-through disc; strong status
  • 💫 Tenet Plinx — hybrid full-auto / charged secondary; high burst
  • 💫 Tenet Exec — heavy blade melee; wide slash; strong with Blood Rush
  • 💫 Tenet Agendus — hammer + shield combo; unique Corpus aesthetic and playstyle
  • 💫 Tenet Spirex / Tenet Tetra / Tenet Livia — solid mid-tier picks

⚡ 4. Elemental Bonuses

How the Bonus Works

Elements
  • Every Kuva and Tenet weapon has an elemental bonus ranging from 25% to 60% of the weapon's total base damage
  • The element is fixed by the Larvling/Candidate spawn — you see it in the preview before mercy killing
  • The bonus is applied before mods — it is a permanent flat multiplier on base damage for that element
  • A 60% bonus is roughly equivalent to a free max-rank elemental mod slot — very significant
  • Element type matters more than bonus % for most weapons — choose your element first, then try to get 50%+ if possible

Element Choice by Faction

Elements
  • Grineer — Radiation (combined) is best; use Heat if running Corrosive + Heat builds
  • Corpus — Magnetic + Toxin (combined) shreds shields + health; Toxin alone bypasses shields
  • Infested — Corrosive or Gas; Corrosive strips armor on Ancients, Gas AoE for clusters
  • General / all factions — Heat is the most flexible bonus element; applies to all factions and synergizes with Viral builds
  • Radiation — strong vs Grineer heavy units and Alloy Armor; also causes friendly fire between enemy factions (CC utility)

Best Element by Weapon Role

Elements
  • Status weapons (Kuva Nukor, Tenet Cycron) — Toxin; combine with Cold mod for Viral, or keep Toxin for direct health damage
  • Crit primaries (Kuva Bramma, Kuva Zarr) — Heat; pairs with Corrosive or Viral depending on target faction
  • Eidolon weapons (Kuva Chakkhurr) — Radiation; Eidolons are weak to Radiation on their body
  • Melee (Tenet Exec) — Heat or Radiation; Heat synergizes with Archon Vitality healing on status procs
  • Universal pick when unsure — Heat; it is never a bad choice and has the widest utility

⚖️ 5. Valence Fusion

What Is Valence Fusion?

Valence
  • A system that lets you combine two copies of the same Kuva or Tenet weapon to increase the elemental bonus
  • The resulting weapon takes the higher bonus % + 2% of the two copies (capped at 60%)
  • You can also change the element type during fusion — keep the element of whichever copy you designate as the base
  • The weapon consumed in the fusion is destroyed — make sure you no longer need it before fusing

Fusion Strategy

Valence
  • Start with any copy; fuse duplicates until you reach 58–60% for your final weapon
  • Use low-bonus copies as sacrificial fodder to raise your keeper weapon's percentage
  • If you get a better element on a new drop, you can swap elements via fusion — designate the new-element copy as the sacrifice to transfer its element to your high-% keeper
  • Trading: you can buy 25% copies cheaply from other players, then fuse them into your keeper to climb toward 60%
  • A 60% Heat Kuva Nukor + maxed status mods is one of the highest damage-per-second secondaries in the game

Fusion Math

Valence
  • Example: 25% + 25% → 27% (higher of the two + 2%)
  • Example: 27% + 30% → 32%
  • Example: 50% + 45% → 52%
  • Example: 56% + 52% → 58%
  • Example: 58% + any% → 60% (cap)
  • Reaching 60% from a 25% start takes roughly 10–15 total fusions depending on luck of drops
  • Each fusion costs no resources — only the sacrificed weapon copy

🏆 6. Top Picks

🔥 Top Kuva Weapons

Top Kuva
  • 🔥 Kuva Nukor (Secondary) — best status secondary in the game; chains to 5 enemies; combine with Epitaph or Tenet Cycron for Viral primer
  • 🔥 Kuva Bramma (Primary) — explosive cluster arrow; top AoE primary for Survival and Defense; watch for self-damage
  • 🔥 Kuva Zarr (Primary) — cannon that fires shells or buckshot; AoE rivaling Bramma; slightly safer to use in tight spaces
  • 🔥 Kuva Chakkhurr (Primary) — single-shot with innate Radiation; top Eidolon primary option
  • 🔥 Kuva Kohm (Primary) — shotgun with stacking fire rate; devastating with max status builds; shreds Corrupted
  • 🔥 Kuva Ogris (Primary) — rocket launcher with Nightwatch Napalm augment; strong fire/heat status output

💫 Top Tenet Weapons

Top Tenet
  • 💫 Tenet Arca Plasmor (Primary) — wide beam shotgun; high status; excellent for Corpus and mixed-faction missions
  • 💫 Tenet Cycron (Secondary) — status beam secondary; pairs with Kuva Nukor as a Viral primer combo
  • 💫 Tenet Flux Rifle (Primary) — continuous beam; extremely high armor strip rate with status stacking; strong on Steel Path
  • 💫 Tenet Diplos (Secondary) — auto-targeting homing pistols; excellent for high-mobility play where aiming is impractical
  • 💫 Tenet Exec (Melee) — heavy blade; wide AoE slam; Blood Rush + Weeping Wounds build is very strong
  • 💫 Tenet Agendus (Melee) — unique hammer/shield; heavy attack on shields; strong and fun playstyle

Farming Priority

Priority
  • Priority 1: Kuva Nukor — single biggest DPS upgrade for your secondary slot; essential for most meta builds
  • Priority 2: Kuva Bramma or Kuva Zarr — covers AoE primary slot; pick Zarr for tight spaces, Bramma for open tiles
  • Priority 3: Tenet Arca Plasmor — best Corpus-faction primary; replaces vanilla Arca in all content
  • Priority 4: Tenet Cycron — strong Viral primer for secondary if you use Nukor as DPS
  • After that: Farm any that fill gaps in your current loadouts; all are worthwhile for MR
  • Tip: check Warframe.market for cheap 25–40% copies — buying rather than farming saves hours for less sought-after weapons

Leveling to Rank 40

Priority
  • Kuva and Tenet weapons can be leveled to Rank 40 (requires 5 additional Forma past Rank 30)
  • Each extra rank-5 threshold gives a small base stat increase — worth doing for weapons you use often
  • You do not need to take a weapon to Rank 40 to use it effectively — Rank 30 with good mods is sufficient for most content
  • Best leveling location: Hydron (Sedna) Defense with an affinity booster active
  • One full Hydron run (wave 20) with a booster can take a weapon from 0 to 30 in ~15 minutes

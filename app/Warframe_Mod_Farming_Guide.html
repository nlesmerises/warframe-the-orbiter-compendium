🃏 Core Mod Acquisition Guide

Where to farm the essential meta mods · Corrupted · Nightmare · Primed · Galvanized · Augments

🔮

Corrupted Mods

Massive stat buffs with penalties. Farm Orokin Vaults on Deimos using Dragon Keys from your Clan Dojo.
💀

Nightmare Mods

Two positive stats each. Drop from Nightmare Missions (red swirling nodes that appear once you clear every node on a planet).
📻

Aura Mods

Increase mod capacity and grant team-wide buffs. Purchased from the Nightwave Store using Nightwave Creds. Store rotates weekly.
⭐

Primed Mods

Upgraded versions with 10 ranks. Purchased from Baro Ki’Teer every two weeks using Ducats + Credits. Some are login milestone rewards.
🌍

Open World Set Mods

Grant a set bonus when equipped together. Farmed from Bounties in Cetus, Fortuna, or the Necralisk.
⚖️

Galvanized & Arbitrations

Endgame mods from the Arbiters of Hexis using Vitus Essence. Complete the full Star Chart to unlock Arbitration missions.
🛡️

Warframe Augments

Change how a Warframe ability works. Purchased from Syndicate Consoles for 25,000 Standing at maximum rank.
🎲

Notable Rare Drops

A few crucial mods drop from very specific enemies with low drop rates.

🔮 1. Corrupted Mods (Orokin Vaults)

How to Farm

Source

These mods offer a massive buff to one stat and a penalty to another. Equip Dragon Keys (blueprints from your Clan Dojo) and open the hidden vaults inside Deimos missions (Horend, Phlegyas).

🃏 Blind Rage

+Strength, -Efficiency

🃏 Transient Fortitude

+Strength, -Duration

🃏 Narrow Minded

+Duration, -Range

🃏 Fleeting Expertise

+Efficiency, -Duration

🃏 Overextended

+Range, -Strength

🃏 Critical Delay

+Crit Chance, -Fire Rate (Rifles)

🃏 Heavy Caliber

+Damage, -Accuracy (Rifles)

🃏 Spoiled Strike

+Damage, -Attack Speed (Melee)

💀 2. Nightmare Mods

How to Farm

Source

Two positive stats each. Drop from Nightmare Missions (red swirling nodes that appear once you clear every node on a planet).

🃏 Lethal Torrent

+Fire Rate, +Multishot (Pistol) — Tier 3

🃏 Constitution

+Duration, +Knockdown Recovery — Tier 3

🃏 Animal Instinct

+Loot Radar, +Enemy Radar (Companion) — Tier 2/3

🃏 Blaze

+Damage, +Heat Damage (Shotgun) — Tier 1

🃏 Vigor

+Health, +Shields — Tier 1

📻 3. Aura Mods (Nightwave)

How to Farm

Source

Auras increase mod capacity and grant team-wide buffs. Almost exclusively purchased from the Nightwave Store (Nora’s Mix) using Nightwave Creds. Store rotates weekly.

🃏 Corrosive Projection

Strips enemy armor (Nightwave Store)

🃏 Energy Siphon

Passive Energy regen (Nightwave Store)

🃏 Steel Charge

+Melee Damage (Nightwave Store)

🃏 Growing Power

+Ability Strength on status proc (Silver Grove Specters, Earth)

🃏 Brief Respite

Convert Energy spent to Shields (Silver Grove Specters, Earth)

⭐ 4. Primed Mods

Baro Ki’Teer

Void Trader

Upgraded versions with 10 ranks. Purchased from Baro Ki’Teer every two weeks using Ducats + Credits. Some are login milestone rewards.

🃏 Primed Continuity

+55% Duration (Baro Ki’Teer)

🃏 Primed Flow

Massive Energy pool (Baro Ki’Teer)

🃏 Primed Reach

+3m Melee Range (Baro Ki’Teer)

🃏 Primed Fury / Shred / Vigor

Login Milestones (Day 200, 400, etc.)

🌍 5. Open World Set Mods

Bounty Rewards

Source

Mods that grant a set bonus when equipped together. Farmed from Bounties in Cetus, Fortuna, or the Necralisk.

🃏 Augur Message / Secrets / Reach

Converts energy to shields — Cetus Bounties

🃏 Hunter Munitions

Forces Slash procs on crits — Ghoul Purge Bounties

🃏 Blood Rush / Weeping Wounds

Combo scales Crit/Status — Deimos Bounties

🃏 Gladiator Might / Vice

Combo scales Crit Chance — Cetus Bounties

⚖️ 6. Galvanized & Rolling Guard (Arbitrations)

Vitus Essence Store

Source

Endgame mods purchased from the Arbiters of Hexis in the Relay using Vitus Essence. Complete the full Star Chart to unlock Arbitration missions.

🃏 Galvanized Chamber / Hell

Stacking Multishot on kill

🃏 Galvanized Aptitude / Savvy

Stacking Damage/Status on kill

🃏 Rolling Guard

Dodging grants 3s Invincibility

🃏 Adaptation

Builds 90% resistance to damage taken (drops directly in Arbitration missions)

🛡️ 7. Warframe Augments

Syndicate Consoles

Source

Mods that change how a Warframe ability works (e.g., Mesa’s Waltz, Path of Statues). Purchased from Syndicate Consoles in your Orbiter for 25,000 Standing at maximum rank.

🃏 All Augment Mods

Available via Steel Meridian, Arbiters of Hexis, Cephalon Suda, Perrin Sequence, Red Veil, or New Loka

🎲 8. Notable Rare Enemy Drops

Enemy Drops

Source

A few crucial mods drop from very specific enemies with low drop rates.

🃏 Condition Overload

+Damage per status type — Deimos Jugulus (Cambion Drift) or Drekar Strike Commanders (Uranus)

🃏 Bite (Kubrow/Kavat)

Massive Pet Crit — Feral Kubrows (Earth) or Drahk Masters

🃏 Berserker Fury

Attack Speed on kill — Corrupted Ancients (The Void)

⚗️ Elemental Combinations Reference

Viral (Cold + Toxin)

Multiplies damage to health by up to +325% at 10 stacks. The meta choice.

Corrosive (Electric + Toxin)

Strips target armor by up to 80% at 10 stacks. Destroys heavy units.

Radiation (Heat + Electric)

Causes targets to attack allies and draw aggro. Bonus vs bosses.

Magnetic (Cold + Electric)

Amplifies damage to shields by up to +325%. Stops shield recharge.

🗝️ Key Mod Combinations

Blood Rush + Organ Shatter

Scales Melee critical rate per combo stack, multiplying massive critical damage.

Condition Overload + Weeping Wounds

Weeping Wounds secures status procs, driving Condition Overload's damage scaling.

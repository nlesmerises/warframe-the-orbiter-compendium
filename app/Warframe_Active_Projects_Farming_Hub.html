⚔️ The Pennant (Heavy Attack Melee)

0% Completed (0/4 Steps)
  • Farm Pennant Blueprint Drops from Grineer Commanders (Captain Karith, Kosma, or Glis) inside Railjack nodes in the Lupus / Flexa (Veil Proxima) sectors.
  • Gather Crafting Components Acquire 8,000 Carbides (Ceres drops), 8,000 Ticor Plate (Railjack drops), and 150 Gallium.
  • Initiate Foundry Crafting Build the Pennant inside your Orbiter Foundry. Consumes 30,000 Credits and requires a 12-hour wait.
  • Slot Melee Critical Build Mods Fully rank up and slot Killing Blow, Corrupt Charge, Sacrificial Steel, and Organ Shatter to unleash the 500k heavy attacks.

🚀 Amesha (Support Archwing)

0% Completed (0/10 Steps)

    📋 Phase 1: Blueprint Acquisition

  • Join a Clan (If Needed) Ensure you are in a Clan that has the Amesha researched in their Tenno Lab.
  • Purchase Blueprints Travel to the Dojo's Tenno Lab and buy the Main, Harness, Systems, and Wings blueprints (~60,000 Credits total).

    • Phase 2: Rare & Time-Gated Resources

  • Acquire 4x Nitain Extract Purchase from the Nightwave Cred Offerings store (15 creds for a pack of 5).
  • Acquire Invasion Resources Obtain 1x Detonite Injector, 1x Mutagen Mass, and 1x Fieldron from Invasions, Fortuna/Cetus bounties, or Dojo crafting.

    • ⛏️ Phase 3: Bulk Resource Farming

  • The "Ophelia" Mega-Farm (Uranus - Survival) Farm here for 3x Tellurium, 5,650x Polymer Bundle, and 1,350x Plastids. Use a farming frame like Nekros or Khora.
  • The Oxium Grind (Jupiter - Io Defense) Farm 2,200x Oxium. Target and destroy Oxium Ospreys before they suicide-dive. Stay for 15-20 waves.
  • The Cryotic Dig (Earth - Everest OR Pluto - Hieracon) Farm 1,250x Cryotic. You must successfully defend 13 complete Excavators (100 per extractor).
  • Basic Resource Cleanup Ensure you have 7,250x Ferrite (Earth/Mercury) and 6,200x Salvage (Mars/Jupiter).

    • 🔨 Phase 4: Crafting

  • Craft Components Start crafting the Harness, Systems, and Wings in the Foundry (72 hours build time).
  • Craft Amesha Main Assembly Claim components and start the final Amesha build (12 hours build time).

🛡️ Voidrig Necramech (Progression Gate)

📆 View Endgame Systems Codex
0% Completed (0/5 Steps)
  • Farm Damaged Necramech Components Kill enemy Necramechs in Isolation Vault bounties (Cambion Drift, Deimos) to acquire the Damaged Necramech Casing, Engine, Capsule, and Weapon Pod.
  • Obtain Blueprints from Father & Loid Purchase the Voidrig Component Blueprints and Main Blueprint from Loid (Necraloid Syndicate) in the Necralisk using Orokin Matrix currency.
  • Mine & Refine Deimos Minerals Refine minerals like Stellated Necrathene, Adramal Alloy, Tempered Bapholite, and Venerol to forge the high-tech components.
  • Craft Necramech Components Craft all four components (Casing, Engine, Capsule, Weapon Pod) in your Foundry (12-hour wait time).
  • Combine Voidrig Main Blueprint Combine the four completed components with your main Voidrig blueprint (72-hour craft time) to gain access to the Steel Path / New War questline!

🧉 1. What Is a Necramech?

🧉 Heavy War-Mech

  • A Necramech is a pilotable heavy mech with its own health, armor, abilities, and Arch-gun — far tankier than a Warframe.
  • Summoned in the field with the Transference gear item once unlocked, then piloted directly.
  • Two models exist: Voidrig (artillery/offense) and Bonewidow (melee/tank).

🎯 Why Build One

  • Required to clear Orphix missions (Sentient suppression) where Warframe powers are disabled.
  • Heavy DPS and survivability for Isolation Vaults, open worlds, and Steel Path.
  • Counts toward Mastery Rank — both mechs and the Mausolon Arch-gun give MR.

🔓 2. How to Unlock Necramechs

📋 Prerequisites

  • Complete the Heart of Deimos quest to access the Necralisk and Cambion Drift.
  • Reach Old Mate rank with the Entrati syndicate to buy the Necramech build prerequisites and Isolation Vault keys.
  • You build the mech in the Foundry from a main blueprint (bought from Mother/Loid) plus four part blueprints that drop.

💡 The Short Version

Run Isolation Vault bounties on Cambion Drift. The Necraweb / Damaged Necramech caches and vault rewards drop the Casing, Capsule, Engine, and Weapon Pod blueprints. Buy the main BP from the Necralisk, farm parts, craft, then claim the mech. Repeat for the second model.

💥 4. Voidrig — The Artillery Mech

🎯 Role

Offensive specialist. Voidrig is the default Orphix and DPS pick — its 4th ability turns it into a stationary cannon that shreds Sentients and bosses.

⚡ Abilities

  • 1 — Necraweb: lob a canister that slows enemies in a web cloud.
  • 2 — Storm Shroud: damage-absorbing shield that converts hits into a retaliation buff.
  • 3 — Gravition Maw: a singularity that pulls and crushes enemies.
  • 4 — Arquebex: deploy twin artillery cannons — massive sustained AoE damage while rooted.

🛡️ 5. Bonewidow — The Melee Tank

🎯 Role

Survivability and crowd control. Bonewidow is a front-line bruiser with an exalted sword and shield and strong self-sustain — better for staying mobile and tanking than peak DPS.

⚡ Abilities

  • 1 — Meathook: impale an enemy, hold it as a shield, then throw it for damage and healing.
  • 2 — Shield Maiden: raise a shield that blocks and reflects, granting big damage reduction.
  • 3 — Firing Line: cone blast that damages and disarms groups.
  • 4 — Exalted Ironbride: summon a massive energy blade for sweeping melee strikes.

⚙️ 6. Modding & Arch-guns

🧩 Necramech Mods

  • Core survivability: Necramech Vitality, Steel Fiber, Redirection.
  • Efficiency/ability uptime: Streamline, Continuity, Intensify.
  • Drop from Necramech enemies in Isolation Vaults; rank them with Endo like normal mods.

🔫 Arch-gun Setup

  • The mech wields an Arch-gun — Mausolon is the easy, strong default.
  • Use Arch-gun mods (Hollow Point, Automatic Trigger, elementals) to scale damage.
  • See the Companions & Vehicles Codex for Archwing/Arch-gun overlap.

🗺️ 7. Where You Use Them

  • Orphix missions — the signature use; Warframe powers are suppressed, so the mech does the work.
  • Isolation Vaults & Cambion Drift — great for farming and bounties.
  • Open worlds & many regular/Steel Path missions — mech use was widened in later updates; summon when Transference is available.
  • Profit-Taker & heavy single-target fights — Voidrig's Arquebex is a burst-down tool.

⬆️ 8. Leveling Tips

  • Necramechs gain affinity from their own kills — level them in Isolation Vaults or Orphix, not by parking them.
  • Rank both the mech and the Mausolon for full Mastery; forma both for serious builds.
  • Build Voidrig first for Orphix/DPS, then Bonewidow for tanky variety.
  • Pair vault runs with conservation, fishing, and bounties on Deimos — see the Open Worlds Guide.

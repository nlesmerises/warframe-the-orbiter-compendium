🧬 1. The Helminth System (Ability Swapping)

Acquired from Son in the Necralisk (Deimos). You feed standard (non-Prime) Warframes to the infested mouth in your Orbiter. It destroys the frame, but permanently unlocks one specific ability from that frame, allowing you to paste it onto any other Warframe you own.

🏆 The Top 5 Subsumed Abilities to Unlock First

👑 1. Roar (From Rhino)

The ultimate universal damage buff. It acts as a faction damage multiplier, meaning it double-dips on status effects like Slash bleeds and Heat burns. Slap this on any frame that lacks a damage buff.

💚 2. Nourish (From Grendel)

Arguably the most broken ability in the game. It heals you, adds massive Viral damage to all your weapons, and acts as an Energy Multiplier (energy orbs give you 3x to 4x as much energy). It solves energy economy issues on any frame.

🌓 3. Gloom (From Sevagoth)

Creates a massive aura around you. Enemies inside are slowed by up to 95% (almost completely frozen in time), and any damage you deal to them instantly heals you. Slap this on Garuda or Nekros to become immortal.

🛡️ 4. Pillage (From Hildryn)

The best armor-strip and survival tool. It fires a massive ring that strips enemy armor/shields and returns it to you as Overshields. It also cleanses status effects from you. Incredible on Mesa or Volt.

💀 5. Terrify (From Nekros)

A cheaper, easier alternative to Pillage for stripping armor. It scares enemies away while instantly removing their armor. If you use the "Creeping Terrify" augment, they are also massively slowed.

📊 Helminth Subsumption Tracker

Feed standard (non-Prime) Warframes to the Helminth in your Orbiter to permanently unlock their signature ability. Once you check a Warframe off as Subsumed, you can safely sell that standard copy from your inventory to free up slots!

💎 3. Archon Shards (Permanent Stat Implants)

Acquired from Weekly Archon Hunts (after The New War) and Netracells. You can implant up to 5 of these shards directly into a Warframe. They provide massive, permanent stat boosts that do not consume any Mod Capacity.

💠 Primary Shards

  • Crimson Shard (Red): Used primarily for +Ability Strength, +Ability Duration, or +Melee Critical Damage. Slapping 5 Crimson Shards for Ability Strength onto a frame saves you from having to use Blind Rage!
  • Amber Shard (Yellow): Used primarily for +Casting Speed. A single Amber Shard on frames with long, locked animations (like Nekros' Terrify or Harrow's abilities) is an absolute game-changer. Also great for +Energy Orb Effectiveness.
  • Azure Shard (Blue): Used primarily for +Armor or +Health. Great for beefing up squishy frames without wasting a mod slot on Vitality.

✨ Fused (Coalescent) Shards

You can fuse two primary shards together in the Helminth to create specialized secondary colors.

  • Emerald Shard (Green = Yellow + Blue): The Armor Stripper. The most popular effect is "+Max Stacks of Corrosive Status." If you put 2 Emerald Shards on a frame, Corrosive status can now fully strip 100% of an enemy's armor (normally capped at 80%). This makes Saryn and Hydroid god-tier.
  • Topaz Shard (Orange = Red + Yellow): Popular for +Secondary Critical Chance upon killing enemies with Heat damage. Incredible on Mesa's Peacemakers.
  • Violet Shard (Purple = Red + Blue): Popular for +Melee Critical Damage (doubled if your energy pool is over 500). Incredible for Kullervo and Atlas.

🔄 4. Incarnon Genesis (The Duviri Circuit)

Acquired in the Steel Path Duviri Circuit. This system allows you to take incredibly old, weak, "mastery fodder" weapons (like the Braton, Lex, or Skana) and fuse them with Void energy, turning them into the strongest weapons in the game.

📋 How it Works

You select a weapon adapter from the weekly Circuit reward path. You attach it to the weapon at Cavalero in the Chrysalith (Zariman). As you complete challenges with the weapon, you unlock an evolutionary skill tree that adds massive base stats (like +20% flat Critical Chance).

🔫 The Best Incarnon Genesis Weapons to Farm:

  • Torid: Turns a poison gas launcher into a chaining red-crit lightning beam. (The best primary in the game).
  • Lex / Dual Toxocyst: Turns simple pistols into massive, ricocheting pocket-shotguns or bullet hoses.
  • Ceramic Dagger / Magistar: These are used almost exclusively as "Stat Sticks" for Atlas and Khora, because their Incarnon perks grant massive flat Critical Chance and Melee Damage buffs that apply to Warframe abilities.

🌐 1. Overview

What Is Warframe 1999?

Overview
  • A standalone open-world hub set in Höllvania, Romania — December 31, 1999
  • Introduced in the Warframe 1999 update (late 2024)
  • Blends 1999 retro-futurism with Warframe's Technocyte/Orokin lore
  • Features two new Warframes: Cyte-09 and Jade
  • New enemy faction: Techrot — Technocyte-infected humans

Why Go Here?

Overview
  • Acquire Cyte-09 and Jade Warframe components
  • Eleanor vendor: exclusive mods, cosmetics, and weapons
  • Arthur vendor: relationship-gated rewards and resources
  • Unique 1999-era resources used in new crafting recipes
  • Story content expanding the Drifter and pre-war Orokin timeline

🔑 2. Access & Unlock

Prerequisites

Access
  • Complete the Whispers in the Walls quest (Deimos / Albrecht's Laboratories)
  • Complete the Jade Shadows quest to unlock Jade
  • Cyte-09 requires completing initial 1999 story missions
  • Mastery Rank requirement: check in-game (approx. MR 5+)

Getting There

Access
  • Access Höllvania from the Navigation star chart — dedicated node
  • Hub area: free-roam open world like Cetus / Fortuna / Necralisk
  • Day/night cycle affects mission availability and resource spawns

🛒 3. Vendors

👩 Eleanor

Vendor
  • Primary standing vendor for Höllvania
  • Sells: mods, weapons, cosmetics, crafting blueprints
  • Standing earned by completing 1999 bounties and missions
  • Standing cap: daily limit applies (scales with Mastery Rank)
  • Rank up to unlock higher-tier items — similar to Ostron/Solaris progression

👨 Arthur

Vendor
  • Relationship-gated vendor — trust/affinity builds over missions
  • Sells unique rewards not available from Eleanor
  • Progress by completing Arthur-specific missions and dialogue events
  • Higher trust unlocks lore items, exclusive cosmetics, and weapon parts

💰 Standing Priority

Priority
  • Cap standing daily before logging off
  • Focus on Eleanor rank-ups first to unlock the full shop
  • Arthur trust increases passively through story — no standing grind needed
  • Check Eleanor’s rotating stock for time-limited cosmetics

🤖 4. New Frames

Cyte-09

Frame
  • Stealth/precision frame introduced with Warframe 1999
  • Playstyle: single-target elimination, cloaking, tactical repositioning
  • Components: obtained from 1999 story missions and Eleanor vendor
  • Check builds codex for meta build once established in community

Jade

Frame
  • Support/damage hybrid introduced in the Jade Shadows update
  • Unique passive tied to the Jade Light mechanic
  • Components: obtained via the Jade Shadows quest and drop tables
  • Strong in cooperative play — buffs allies and debuffs enemies simultaneously

🎯 5. Missions & Activities

Bounties

Activity
  • Primary standing source — pick up from Eleanor in the Höllvania hub
  • 5 tiers from low to high level — higher tiers give more standing and better drops
  • Rotate every few hours — check before daily cap to maximize efficiency

Free-Roam Activities

Activity
  • Patrol zones with Techrot enemy camps — source of 1999 resources
  • Timed events during specific day/night phases
  • Hidden caches and lore fragments scattered across Höllvania

Story Missions

Activity
  • Linear quest chain unlocks hub features, vendors, and new frames
  • Complete in order — later missions gate crafting blueprints
  • Replay available after completion for lore review

☠️ 6. Techrot Enemies

Faction Overview

Enemies
  • Techrot: Technocyte-infected humans from the 1999 era
  • Blend biological and mechanical traits — unique weak points
  • Vulnerable to Slash and Viral for flesh components
  • Shielded variants: use Magnetic or Electricity to strip shields first

Notable Enemy Types

Enemies
  • Techrot Grunts — standard infantry, moderate threat
  • Techrot Heavies — armored, high HP — use Corrosive or Radiation
  • Techrot Eximus — buffed variants with auras — priority targets
  • Techrot Bosses — mission-specific, unique mechanics per encounter

🏆 7. Key Rewards

From Eleanor (Standing)

Rewards
  • Cyte-09 Warframe blueprints (components)
  • 1999-era weapon blueprints
  • Exclusive mods and augments
  • Höllvania cosmetics: syandanas, armor sets, glyphs

From Missions & Bounties

Rewards
  • 1999 resources: used in all 1999 crafting recipes
  • Jade Warframe components (Jade Shadows quest chain)
  • Mods dropping from Techrot enemies
  • Relic drops from high-tier bounties

From Arthur (Trust)

Rewards
  • Unique cosmetic rewards not sold by Eleanor
  • Lore items and codex entries
  • Exclusive weapon skins at max trust

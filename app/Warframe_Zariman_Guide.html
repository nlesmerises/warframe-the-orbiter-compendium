☀️ 1. Overview

What Is the Zariman?

Overview
  • A derelict Orokin colony ship lost in the Void — introduced in Angels of the Zariman update
  • Open-world hub distinct from Plains, Fortuna, Deimos — set inside a drifting space vessel
  • The ship where the original Tenno children were irradiated by the Void
  • Features unique indoor/outdoor environments: Chrysalith (hub), Tileset missions, Void spaces

Why Farm Here?

Rewards
  • Arcane Energize, Arcane Guardian, Arcane Blessing — top-tier arcanes from Angels
  • Unique Void weapons: Hespar, Verdilac, Bubonico blueprint variants
  • Holdfast standing for exclusive mods, cosmetics, and Amp parts
  • Voidplume resources for top-tier crafting
  • Gyre Warframe components drop here

🔑 2. Access

Prerequisites

Required
  • Complete The New War quest
  • Complete Angels of the Zariman quest (unlocks the hub and missions)
  • Operator must be available — Void Mode used heavily here

Getting There

Navigation
  • Navigate to Chrysalith (the Zariman hub relay) from the star chart
  • Chrysalith functions like Cetus or Fortuna — pick up bounties, visit vendors
  • Mission nodes depart from Chrysalith into the ship tileset or Void spaces

👥 3. Holdfast Syndicate

Earning Standing

Standing
  • Complete Zariman bounties and mission objectives
  • Collect Voidplumes during missions — turn in to Quinn for bonus standing
  • Daily standing cap applies (scales with Mastery Rank)
  • Cap standing every session before logging off

Quinn — Primary Vendor

Vendor
  • Sells Amp parts, mods, weapon blueprints, cosmetics at each rank
  • Rank up: Fallen → Watcher → Vessel → Speaker → Instrument
  • Higher ranks unlock Gyre blueprints and exclusive Zariman weapons

Voidplumes

Resource
  • Collectible items found during missions in hidden spots
  • 4 tiers: Vane (low) → Quill → Crest → Flourish (high)
  • Higher tiers give more standing per turn-in — prioritize Crest and Flourish
  • Marked on minimap when Void Mode is active — use Operator to spot them

🎯 4. Mission Types

Bounties (Chrysalith)

Bounties
  • 5-tier bounties from Quinn — primary standing source
  • Each bounty has 3 stages; completing all stages gives bonus rewards
  • Higher tiers drop better arcanes and Voidplumes
  • Rotate every few hours — refresh timing syncs with other open worlds

Void Flood

Mission
  • Defend Vitoplast containers from Void Manifestations
  • Collect Vitoplast scattered around the map to repair containers
  • Operator Void Mode useful for navigating quickly between objectives
  • Efficient standing and Voidplume source

Void Armageddon

Mission
  • Defend Reliquary Drive nodes from endless enemy waves
  • Survival-style: stay as long as possible for better rewards
  • Angels of the Zariman (boss enemies) spawn at higher rotations
  • Best arcane drop source at Rotation C+

🐎 5. Void Cascade

How It Works

Mechanics
  • Purge Exolizers from Void corruption before they expire
  • Exolizers must be cleansed by standing in them with Operator Void Mode active
  • Cascades spawn more Exolizers as the mission progresses — manage multiple at once
  • Failing to cleanse causes a cascade failure — resets the current chain

Tips

Strategy
  • Bring a fast Warframe (Volt, Nova, Wisp) to cover ground quickly
  • Squad of 4 is ideal — split between cleansing and enemy control
  • Vazarin Focus gives Operator healing while cleansing — highly recommended
  • Exolizer locations marked on minimap — learn the common spawn clusters

👻 6. Angels & Enemies

Angels of the Zariman (Boss)

Boss
  • Massive Void-corrupted Tenno entities — primary arcane source
  • Weak point: the glowing Void core on their chest — shoot it to knock them down
  • Destroy all 4 limb weak points before the core becomes vulnerable
  • Use Operator Void Mode to avoid instant-kill beams
  • Drop top-tier arcanes (Energize, Guardian, Blessing, etc.) at high quantities

Void Manifestations

Enemies
  • Standard Zariman enemies — corrupted Grineer/Corpus hybrids
  • Damage types: use Viral + Slash for flesh units
  • Eximus variants spawn frequently — they have energy auras, kill them first
  • Battalyst and Symbilyst: heavy units, use high-damage single-target weapons

🏆 7. Key Rewards

Top Arcanes (from Angels)

Arcanes
  • Arcane Energize — energy on pickup, essential for energy-hungry builds
  • Arcane Guardian — armor buff on damage taken, top survival arcane
  • Arcane Blessing — max health buff on pickup
  • Arcane Avenger — critical chance on damage taken
  • All drop at all ranks (R0) — farm 21× per arcane to max (R5)

From Quinn (Standing)

Vendor
  • Gyre Warframe blueprints (Neuroptics, Chassis, Systems)
  • Amp parts: Prism, Scaffold, Brace components exclusive to Zariman
  • Unique Zariman weapons: Hespar, Verdilac
  • Exclusive cosmetics: ephemeras, syandanas, armor

Voidplume Resources

Resource
  • Used in Zariman weapon and Gyre crafting recipes
  • Also turned in directly for standing
  • Farm during bounties — activate Operator Void Mode to detect them

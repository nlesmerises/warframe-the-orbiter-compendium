⏳️ Weekly Reset Checklist (Sunday Resets)

Core Concept
Warframe's most important vendors reset on Sunday at 00:00 UTC. Visit these NPCs every week to buy their limited stock.
Weekly Reset
Sunday 00:00 UTC — the anchor point for every vendor below.
Don't Miss
Bird 3's Archon Shard is mandatory every week — the single highest-priority weekly buy.

⚔️ Teshin — Any Relay

Currency: Steel Essence
  • Sells 1 rotating high-value item every week (Umbra Forma, 3x Forma, or Kuva).
  • Sells 35,000 Kuva weekly.
  • Sells 1 Riven Cipher weekly (instantly unlocks a Riven).

🙏 Palladino — Iron Wake, Earth

Currency: Riven Slivers
  • Sells exactly 2 veiled Riven Mods per week.
  • Sells 35,000 Kuva per week.
  • Sells credits.

🌀 Acrithis — Duviri / Dormizone

Currency: Pathos Clamps
  • Sells 1 Orokin Catalyst or Reactor per week.
  • Sells rotating Arcanes.
  • Sells 100,000 Kuva per week.

⚡ Cavalero & Bird 3 — Zariman / Sanctum

Currency: Standing / Resource Tokens
  • Cavalero (Zariman) sells weekly Voidplumes.
  • Bird 3 (Sanctum Anatomica) sells 1 Archon Shard per week — mandatory to buy every week.

💎 Baro Ki'Teer — The Void Trader

Core Concept
Baro is the most important vendor in the game. He arrives exactly every 2 weeks on Friday at 14:00 UTC and stays for exactly 48 hours.
Currency & Inventory
Ducats Credits He brings a random assortment of Primed Mods, Prisma Weapons, and cosmetics.
Tracking
You cannot predict what he will bring — check Warframe Hub or log in the moment he arrives to see his stock.

🎯 Ducat Farming Tips

  • Run Void Fissure missions and pick only Prime parts you don’t need — sell the extras to Baro’s kiosks at any relay
  • Ducat kiosks are at each relay — no need to wait for Baro’s visit
  • Check warframe.market before selling — some parts are worth more in Platinum than Ducats
  • Axi and Neo fissures drop the highest-value Prime parts for Ducats
  • Baro typically requires 200–800 Ducats per item plus Credits

⚔️ Steel Path Teshin — Weekly Offerings

🛍 Daily Vendors (Darvo & Cephalon Simaris)

Core Concept
Two daily resets — one worth skipping most of the time, one worth checking every day.

🛍 Darvo — Any Relay

Daily resetUsually a trap

Sells one weapon or item for Platinum every 24 hours at a massive discount. Weapons can be farmed for free — don't spend platinum here unless it's a cosmetic or a Catalyst/Reactor.

📡 Cephalon Simaris — Any Relay

Static inventoryDaily standing cap

Sells specific blueprints, Exilus Adapters, and mods. Inventory never changes, but Standing earned toward him is capped per day.

🔗 Live Tracking Tools (External)

Core Concept
Because Warframe operates on real-world timers, you cannot track live inventories offline — use community APIs to check their status.

🌐 Warframe Hub

Pulls live API data directly from game servers. Tracks Cetus cycles, fissures, active sorties, and Baro countdowns.

📱 Official Companion App

Allows remote login to deploy resource extractors, start foundry builds, and check Baro's active inventory.

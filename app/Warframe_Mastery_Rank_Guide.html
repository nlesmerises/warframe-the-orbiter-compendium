📜 2. The Math of Mastery Points

To rank up quickly, you must prioritize the items that give the most points. Always have these building in your Foundry.

🏆 High Value (6,000 Points Each)

  • Warframes (Prime or Base)
  • Companions (Sentinels, Kavats, Kubrows, Moas)
  • Archwings & Necramechs
  • K-Drives (from Fortuna)

⭐ Standard Value (3,000 Points Each)

  • Primary Weapons
  • Secondary Weapons
  • Melee Weapons
  • Archguns & Archmelees
  • Sentinel Weapons

💜 The Secret Goldmine: Intrinsics

Every single Intrinsic point you spend in Railjack or The Duviri Paradox gives you 1,500 Mastery Points. Maxing out your Duviri and Railjack trees will instantly give you hundreds of thousands of Mastery Points without having to craft a single weapon!

📌 3. The Best Farming Locations

Do not level weapons in normal missions. It takes too long. Use these three dedicated XP farming nodes.

🏆 1. Elite Sanctuary Onslaught (ESO)

Best for: Leveling Weapons (Insanely Fast)
Location: Cephalon Simaris (Syndicate Menu)

  • This is the fastest weapon XP farm in the game.
  • The Trick: Unequip all weapons except the ONE you want to level. If you only bring one weapon, it absorbs 75% of all shared Affinity from your teammates.
  • It takes 2-3 zones to max a weapon from 0 to 30.
  • Note: You cannot bring Warframes under rank 30 into ESO.

⚖️ 2. Normal Sanctuary Onslaught (SO)

Best for: Leveling Warframes
Location: Cephalon Simaris

  • Because you can't bring under-leveled frames into Elite, you bring them to regular SO.
  • Cast your abilities as much as possible, as Warframe ability kills give 100% of the Affinity directly to the Warframe.

🌐 3. Hydron (Sedna) or Helene (Saturn)

Best for: Casual leveling & resource farming.

  • These are tiny Grineer Defense maps. Players constantly run them to level up.
  • Helene (Saturn) is mathematically better because it drops Orokin Cells, which you need hundreds of to build Prime weapons.

🔨 4. The “Foundry Pipeline” Strategy

The biggest mistake players make is waiting until they need MR to start building weapons. By the time you need it, you are stuck behind 12-24 hour crafting timers.

  • Step 1 (The Dojo Raid): Go to your Clan Dojo. Visit the Tenno Lab, Energy Lab, Bio Lab, and Chem Lab. Buy the blueprints for every single weapon you do not own.
  • Step 2 (The Market Sweep): Open the ship Market. Check the "Weapons" tab. Check the box for "Blueprints Only." Buy every blueprint that costs Credits.
  • Step 3 (The Pipeline): Craft 10-15 weapons at once. Do not claim them. The Foundry has infinite storage space. Leave them sitting in the Foundry as finished items.
  • Step 4 (The Grind): When you get an Affinity Booster (from daily login or Baro), claim 3 "fodder" weapons from your foundry, run ESO for 10 minutes to max them to 30, sell them for credits, and immediately claim 3 more.

🔎 5. Easy-to-Miss Mastery Points

  • The Star Chart: Completing the normal Star Chart and The Steel Path Star Chart gives roughly 55,000 Mastery Points.
  • Kitguns & Zaws: You only get Mastery for the "Chamber" (Kitgun) or "Strike" (Zaw). Furthermore, you do NOT get the points when you hit rank 30 the first time. You must level it to 30, "Gild" it at the vendor, and then level it to 30 a second time to get the points.
  • Modular Pets: Moas and Predasites work exactly like Zaws. You must level them to 30, Gild them, and level them again to get the mastery points.
  • Kuva & Tenet Weapons: These go up to Rank 40. You must put 5 Forma into them (ranking them to max 5 times) to get all 4,000 Mastery Points out of them.
